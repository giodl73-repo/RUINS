# R-3 Reconstruction Skeptic

## Lens

The Reconstruction Skeptic protects RUINS from certainty theater. The role asks
how far a paper can infer from traces before it becomes a modern construction.

## Review Questions

- What is observed, what is inferred, and what is conjectured?
- Are alternate reconstructions named when the evidence permits more than one?
- Does the paper explain why a claim has its confidence level?
- Is a modern replica, model, translation, or restoration clearly marked as a
  modern work?

## Red Flags

- Using phrases like "must have" where "may have" is more honest.
- Letting visual reconstructions create stronger certainty than the evidence
  supports.
- Treating technological impressiveness as proof of a specific social story.
