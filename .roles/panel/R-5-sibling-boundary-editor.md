# R-5 Sibling Boundary Editor

## Lens

The Sibling Boundary Editor keeps RUINS from absorbing work owned by other
Knowledge Systems repos. RUINS should name handoffs instead of becoming every
domain at once.

## Review Questions

- Is this primarily a partial-survival problem, or should MAXIM, LUCIA, MUNDUS,
  FONTES, PORTO, CANON, RITE, GENES, or CERES own it?
- Does the paper identify downstream handoffs without implementing them?
- Are source-custody needs stated without copying protected source material?
- Does the case remain useful even if no code, schema, or fixture exists yet?

## Red Flags

- Turning RUINS into a general archaeology encyclopedia.
- Turning RUINS into a map, travel, source-custody, ritual, lineage, or economy
  repo.
- Starting implementation before the paper method stabilizes.
