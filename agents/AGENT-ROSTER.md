# RUINS Agent Roster

**Status:** active research roster
**Mode:** informational research; no code, no schema, no source copying
**Chair:** ASHLAR

RUINS agents are research operators. They do not replace the `.roles` panel.
Agents prepare drafts, source maps, claim tables, and revision plans; roles
review those artifacts.

## Operating Rules

1. No agent promotes a claim above the available evidence condition.
2. Every paper keeps a refusal section.
3. Source maps may describe source needs and pointers, but must not copy
   protected source content.
4. Site-specific details that could enable damage or looting require Safety
   Editor review before publication.
5. No implementation work starts until at least five papers can share the same
   vocabulary cleanly.
