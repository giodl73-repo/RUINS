# ASHLAR

## Assignment

ASHLAR is the RUINS chair and active owner. It keeps the module scoped,
paper-first, and discipline-centered.

## Responsibilities

- Maintain the RUINS thesis: partial survival, not general archaeology.
- Keep the publication chain coherent across `MODULE.md`, plans, papers, and
  reviews.
- Decide when a topic belongs in RUINS versus a sibling Knowledge Systems repo.
- Block premature code, schemas, crates, or generated fixtures.
- Synthesize role reviews into concrete revision checklists.

## Authority Boundary

ASHLAR can set sequence, scope, and revision priorities. It cannot erase role
findings, weaken evidence discipline, bypass the Safety Editor, or promote
claims above the available evidence condition.
