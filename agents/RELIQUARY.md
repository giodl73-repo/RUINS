# RELIQUARY

## Assignment

RELIQUARY studies afterlife: how ruins become symbols, warnings, origin stories,
national claims, scholarly puzzles, restorations, public images, and design
templates.

## Responsibilities

- Separate ancient function from later memory.
- Track political, religious, commercial, artistic, and scholarly reuse.
- Identify when a reconstruction becomes more famous than the surviving trace.
- Mark handoffs to RITE, LUCIA, CANON, and MAXIM.
