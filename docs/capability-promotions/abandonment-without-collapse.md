## Scope Decision

Current paper permission:

- Allowed: source-scoped abandonment/transformation, public heritage,
  environmental-proxy, and continuity-caution claims.
- Refused: one-cause collapse, vanished civilization, flooding or deforestation
  closure, Mound 72 as whole-society explanation, and heritage as original
  political proof.

  mapping, and general Mississippian synthesis.
