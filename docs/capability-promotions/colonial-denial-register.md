## Overclaim Pressure

Colonial denial can re-enter a paper as "mystery," non-local authorship, trade
goods as origin proof, or heritage memory mistaken for medieval identity.

## Control Artifacts

| Artifact | Path | What it controls |

| Cross-case checklist | `docs/colonial-denial-register-checklist.md` | Makes the shared material/authorship/denial/memory controls reusable. |

## ASHLAR Decision

Record `colonial-denial-register` as Core. Great Zimbabwe and Mound Builder
denial now show that the same register works across different monumental
traditions and colonial settings. The Core method does not turn RUINS into a
general history repo. It is a scoring and refusal rule: colonial, racialized,
settler, lost-race, foreign-builder, or ownerless-history claims must be
identified as denial/memory layers unless separately source-locked as
historiography.
