## Problem

Cryptotephra can connect archaeological and environmental archives with unusual
precision, but microscopic ash is not a solved chronology. Glass shards,
geochemical fingerprints, sulfate spikes, ice-core/lake-core/marine-core
correlations, radiocarbon ranges, and public Santorini/Thera stories can be
collapsed into exact eruption dates, certain source attribution, direct
destruction proof, complete cultural synchronization, climate causation, or
collapse narratives.

## Core Rule

Cryptotephra claims must keep shard, context, geochemical fingerprint, eruption
source, age model, stratigraphic position, redeposition, archive correlation,
chronological transfer, event claim, sibling synthesis, and public catastrophe
memory separate.

## Required Controls

- Cryptotephra gate.
- Source lock.
- Page-anchor audit.
- Refusal register.
- Cultural-memory register.
- Sibling-boundary register.
- Cross-case checklist.

## Decision

Promote directly to Core under rubric v1.48. The Santorini/Thera chronology case
creates a reusable control for microscopic volcanic marker horizons, archive
correlation, and eruption/collapse memory.
