## ASHLAR Decision

Record `deliberate-textual-destruction` as Core. Maya codices and Qin book
burning show that deliberate destruction is distinct from broader
destroyed-corpus custody: it needs policy/report custody, target categories,
exemptions, survival layers, source mediation, and memory-image controls before
it can support publication claims.
