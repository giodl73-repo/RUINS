# Dental Calculus Reconstruction Custody

**Capability id:** `ruins-capability:dental-calculus-reconstruction-custody`
**Promotion:** Candidate -> Active
**Trigger paper:** `research/publications/panel-neanderthal-dental-calculus-custody/`
**Date:** 2026-07-07

## Pressure

Dental calculus preserves food, microbial, host, environmental, and contaminant
signals in one small matrix. Public summaries can quickly convert taxonomic
hits into exact meals, medicine, disease, intimacy, or lifestyle claims.

## Activation Rule

Activate this capability when a paper reconstructs diet, health, oral
microbiome, pathogen exposure, plant use, medicine, behaviour, or ecology from
dental calculus DNA, proteins, microremains, isotopes, or residue chemistry.

## Required Gate

Separate specimen, calculus formation, sampling, contamination, sequencing or
assay method, taxonomic assignment, oral microbiome, dietary residue, pathogen
signal, behavioural inference, and public memory.

## Refusals

- A taxonomic DNA hit is not an exact meal.
- A plant-associated signal is not deliberate medicine by itself.
- A microbiome profile is not a complete health diagnosis.
- A few specimens are not a species-wide lifestyle.
- Public diet headlines are not final archaeological conclusions.

## Decision

Promote to Active. Queue an unlike second calculus case before Core promotion.
