## Overclaim Pressure

A working model, replica, imaging reconstruction, or functional explanation can
be mistaken for the surviving artifact itself or for final certainty about the
ancient device.

## Scope Decision

Current paper permission:

- Allowed: source-scoped high-level artifact, imaging, reconstruction, and
  public-memory layer claims.
- Refused: model equals object, all gears known, front display solved as final,
  item-level dataset claims without audit, and broad production-tradition claims.
- Future P3: detailed gear-train layout, inscription readings, item-level NYU
  data, and craft/production ecology.
- Sibling handoff: CANON for part identity; FONTES / MUNDUS for source images
  and inscriptions; PORTO for wreck geography; CERES for craft economy.
