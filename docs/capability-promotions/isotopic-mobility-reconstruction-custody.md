# Isotopic Mobility Reconstruction Custody

**Capability id:** `ruins-capability:isotopic-mobility-reconstruction-custody`
**Promotion:** Candidate -> Active
**Trigger paper:** `research/publications/panel-egtved-girl-isotopic-mobility-custody/`
**Date:** 2026-07-07

## Pressure

Isotope studies can turn small sample sets into vivid life stories: birthplace,
childhood, travel, diet, clothing provenance, trade, and social identity.
RUINS needs a gate between chemical signal, tissue formation, baseline mapping,
mobility inference, and public biography.

## Activation Rule

Activate this capability when a paper reconstructs origin, movement, diet, or
provenance from strontium, oxygen, carbon, nitrogen, sulfur, lead, or related
isotope evidence in human remains, animal remains, textiles, objects, or soils.

## Required Gate

Separate sample, tissue/material formation window, preservation, contamination,
baseline/isoscape, comparison set, mobility inference, identity claim, and
public memory.

## Refusals

- Isotope mismatch is not an exact birthplace.
- Mobility signal is not ethnicity, identity, or biography.
- Baseline maps are not route maps.
- Textile provenance is not personal travel by itself.
- Public origin stories are not final archaeological conclusions.

## Decision

Promote to Active. Queue an unlike second isotope case before Core promotion.
