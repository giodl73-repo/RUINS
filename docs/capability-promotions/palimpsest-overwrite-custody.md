## Overclaim Pressure

An erased and overwritten manuscript can be treated as if imaging simply
restores the original text. In practice, undertext, overtext, scraped
parchment, missing leaves, mold, forged paintings, conservation work, imaging
modality, post-processing, scholar reading, transcription, and publication
status are separate evidence layers.

## ASHLAR Decision

Record `palimpsest-overwrite-custody` as Core. Archimedes and Sinai prove the
same discipline across unlike conditions: a single famous codex with unique
undertexts and forged/damaged leaves, and a collection-scale monastery project
with many manuscripts, languages, imaging workflows, catalogue records, and
preliminary discoveries. The Core method separates material survival,
undertext, overtext, conservation, imaging, processing, reading, catalogue,
edition, and public-memory layers.
