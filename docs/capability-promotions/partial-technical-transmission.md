## Overclaim Pressure

A partly documented technique can be reduced to a secret recipe, universal
method, modern construction guidance, or proof of civilizational superiority.

## ASHLAR Decision

Record `partial-technical-transmission` as Core. Roman concrete and Tyrian
purple now show that the same controls work across distinct technical domains:
building material and biological dye. The Core method is not permission to
publish a complete ancient technique. It is a scoring and refusal rule: no text,
sample, material trace, experiment, public summary, or reconstruction may stand
in for the whole technique without explicit custody.
