## Overclaim Pressure

A well-preserved human body can feel like a direct biography. Face, skin, hair,
gut contents, forensic injuries, radiocarbon date, and display can be collapsed
into identity, final emotion, motive, ritual meaning, social role, or population
history.

## ASHLAR Decision

Record `preserved-body-taphonomy-custody` as Core. Tollund Man and Lindow Man
prove the same discipline across unlike preserved-body conditions: a Danish bog
body with conserved head, gut-content reanalysis, and hanging claim; and a
British bog body with catalogued tissue parts, violent-death ambiguity,
polyethylene-glycol/freeze-drying conservation, and display history. The Core
method separates taphonomy, recovery, forensic claim, conservation, sampling,
display, identity, death cause, motive, and public memory.
