## Overclaim Pressure

A literary tradition can overlap with archaeological evidence and then be
misread as site identity, route reconstruction, event ledger, place-name proof,
or evidence for named actors at the site.

## Scope Decision

Current paper permission:

- Allowed: L'Anse aux Meadows as confirmed Norse site; AD 1021 activity when
  source-scoped; correspondence between site remains and saga traditions.
- Refused: "L'Anse aux Meadows equals Vinland" as default, exact saga
  quotation, named actors, route reconstruction, event ledger, and place-name
  identity without dedicated custody.
- Future P3: saga edition/translation custody, route/name custody, named actor
  custody, and geography/event claims.
- Sibling handoff: PORTO for routes/geography; MAXIM / LUCIA for Norse history;
  CANON for site/entity identity.
