# Cast Void Identity Checklist

Status: core RUINS method
Capability: `ruins-capability:cast-void-identity-custody`
Owner: ASHLAR / PALIMPSEST / ERRATA

Use this checklist when a vanished living actor is known through a negative
trace: body cavity, footprint, trackway, handprint, cast, mold, scan, or
impression.

## Required Separation

| Layer | Required question | RUINS treatment |
|---|---|---|
| Substrate / matrix | What ash, sediment, mud, tuff, fill, or cavity preserved the trace? | Preservation layer, not actor identity. |
| Negative trace | What void, footprint, track, or impression actually survives? | Direct trace at audited scope. |
| Cast / model / scan | Was the trace filled, cast, scanned, photographed, modeled, or copied? | Mediation layer, not original event. |
| Biological attribution | What taxon, sex, age, ancestry, or body form is inferred? | Graded inference; no identity certainty without custody. |
| Movement / posture | What gait, pose, direction, stride, or body position is supported? | Limited behavior signal, not full life story. |
| Group relation | Are multiple traces interpreted as family, pair, group, or coexistence? | Requires separate spatial, temporal, biological, and source custody. |
| Display / restoration | Has display, reburial, cleaning, restoration, scanning, or copying changed access? | Afterlife layer. |
| Public memory | What origin, tragedy, ancestor, or "first step" story attached to the trace? | Reception layer, not proof of every claim. |

## Mandatory Refusals

A cast-void paper must refuse:

1. impression as intact body or complete actor;
2. print shape as species certainty without comparative custody;
3. posture, footprint, or trackway as emotion or intention;
4. proximity as family, pair, or social relation without separate custody;
5. cast, scan, photo, or model as original trace equivalent;
6. sampled individuals or tracks as whole-population history;
7. public origin-story image as direct evidence.

## Cross-Case Test

| Test | Pompeii casts | Laetoli footprints | Core rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| Trace type | Body-shaped voids filled by plaster/resin. | Footprints and trackways in volcanic ash/tuff. | Negative traces require substrate and mediation custody. |
| Actor pressure | Named victims, families, gender, emotion, death tableau. | Species, gait, group, "first human steps" origin story. | Human-like traces invite identity overclaim. |
| Mediation layer | Casts, restoration, display, scans, 3D copies, DNA sampling. | Excavation, cleaning, photogrammetry, laser scanning, casts/photos. | Access technologies are not the original event. |
| Overclaim pressure | Final moment equals biography. | Footprint equals species certainty and full locomotor story. | Refuse total identity, social relation, and population closure. |

## Automatic Cap

A paper cannot score above `34` if it treats a body void, footprint, trackway,
cast, scan, photo, or model as complete identity, species certainty, gait
reconstruction, social relation, emotional state, or population history without
this checklist or an equivalent cast-void custody gate.
