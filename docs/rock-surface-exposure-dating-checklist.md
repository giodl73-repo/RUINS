# Rock-Surface Exposure Dating Checklist

Use this checklist when monument construction date, quarrying date, carving
date, petroglyph age, abandonment, surface stability, erosion rate, boulder
movement, wall age, cairn age, path use, landform association, or public
oldest-surface memory is reconstructed from cosmogenic nuclides, 10Be, 26Al,
36Cl, exposure ages, burial ages, lichenometry, lichen thallus size, growth
curves, rock varnish, varnish microlamination, microerosion, weathering rind,
surface roughness, calibration surfaces, shielding corrections, inheritance,
erosion, or post-exposure disturbance.

## Required Separations

| Surface | Required custody question |
|---|---|
| Sample surface | Which rock face, boulder, wall stone, carving surface, varnish patch, or lichen population is measured? |
| Exposure event | Is the target quarrying, carving, construction, abandonment, erosion, fall, movement, or natural exposure? |
| Method | Is the evidence cosmogenic nuclide, lichenometry, rock varnish, microerosion, or weathering rind? |
| Inheritance | Could prior exposure, inherited nuclides, old lichens, reused stones, or pre-existing varnish affect the age? |
| Shielding | Are topography, snow, sediment, vegetation, burial, and architecture shielding separately modelled? |
| Erosion | Are surface loss, spalling, cleaning, weathering, vandalism, and conservation separated? |
| Calibration | Which growth curve, production model, varnish calibration, or known-age surface supports the date? |
| Association | Is the dated surface directly tied to the archaeological event or only nearby/compatible? |
| Model | Is the result minimum age, exposure age, burial age, relative age, or broad constraint? |
| Memory | Are oldest monument, direct carving date, solved petroglyph age, and cultural phase stories contained? |

## Refuse Unless Separately Locked

- Exposure age is construction date.
- Lichen diameter is exact wall age.
- Rock varnish layer is petroglyph date.
- Cosmogenic age is abandonment event.
- Weathering rind is cultural phase.
- Calibration curve is universal.
- Shielding correction is solved context.
- Surface stability is complete site chronology.
