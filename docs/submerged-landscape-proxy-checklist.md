# Submerged Landscape Proxy Checklist

Status: Core RUINS checklist
Trigger cases: Doggerland; Atlit-Yam
Capability: `ruins-capability:submerged-landscape-proxy-custody`

Use this checklist when a drowned landscape, settlement, or coastal site is
known through underwater survey, seabed exposure, cores, displaced finds,
in-situ excavation, organic remains, human remains, sea-level models, or public
reconstructions.

## Evidence Layers

| Layer | Ask | Blocks |
|---|---|---|
| Bathymetry/topography | Is this seabed or former landform evidence? | Settlement layout, route, or boundary certainty. |
| Geophysical/seismic survey | Is the interpretation direct, modeled, or inferred? | Occupation without independent archaeological context. |
| Sediment/core evidence | What exact sample, depth, and method anchors the claim? | Whole-region ecology, culture, or population history. |
| Displaced artefacts | Was the find trawled, dredged, beach-recovered, or in situ? | Site plans and activity areas from loose finds. |
| In-situ structures | Which feature was excavated and documented underwater? | Complete village plan, social system, or ritual certainty. |
| Organic/faunal/botanical remains | Is the claim economic, environmental, dietary, or preservation-specific? | Complete subsistence system without context and quantification. |
| Human remains | What does the osteological or medical source actually support? | Population identity, ancestry, disease story, or biography. |
| Sea-level process | Is submergence modeled, observed, dated, or narrated? | Single catastrophe, moral lesson, or climate analogy. |
| Public reconstruction | Is this a visualization, model, exhibit, or media phrase? | Original-state proof. |

## Required Refusals

- Refuse lost-civilization framing unless polity/urban claims are separately
  source-locked.
- Refuse route, migration, and ancestry claims from geography or environment
  alone.
- Refuse complete settlement history from either proxy maps or isolated in-situ
  features.
- Refuse disaster-event storytelling unless event evidence is independently
  locked.
- Refuse public reconstructions as original-state evidence.
