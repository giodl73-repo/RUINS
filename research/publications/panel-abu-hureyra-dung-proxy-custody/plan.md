# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use dung spherulites and related proxies without turning proxy
concentrations into complete herding, domestication, or fuel-economy history?

## Scope

- Use the Abu Hureyra PLOS ONE paper as the primary trigger.
- Use multi-method archaeological dung and fecal biomarker papers as pressure.
- Separate deposit, proxy, preservation, host/source, formation, reuse, method,
  activity inference, scale, and memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No direct domestication claim from spherulites alone.
- No exact herd location or penning system without contextual custody.
- No full fuel economy or foddering regime.
- No origin-of-herding story without sibling-owned synthesis.
