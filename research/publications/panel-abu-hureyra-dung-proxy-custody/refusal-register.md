# Refusal Register

- Do not treat dung spherulites as direct domestication.
- Do not treat proxy concentration as exact herd location.
- Do not infer a complete penning system from one proxy class.
- Do not turn dung fuel evidence into full fuel economy.
- Do not turn dung in plaster into complete construction practice.
- Do not treat fecal biomarkers as final species identity without source controls.
- Do not let earliest-herding headlines become origin history.
