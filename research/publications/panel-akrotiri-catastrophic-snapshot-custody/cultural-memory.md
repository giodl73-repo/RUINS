# Cultural Memory: Akrotiri

Akrotiri's public afterlife often turns on preservation spectacle: Pompeii of
the Aegean, lost city, vanished people, Atlantis, and frozen time.

## Memory Surfaces

| Memory surface | RUINS handling |
|---|---|
| Pompeii of the Aegean | Official/public comparison; not evidence equivalence. |
| Frozen city | Reception phrase; not complete final moment. |
| Vanished people | Public mystery; not evacuation certainty. |
| Atlantis | Mythic afterlife, not archaeological claim. |
| Minoan collapse | Broader historical synthesis, not current RUINS scope. |

## Rule

Public memory may explain why Akrotiri is compelling. It may not fill missing
final-moment, evacuation, ritual, or civilizational evidence.
