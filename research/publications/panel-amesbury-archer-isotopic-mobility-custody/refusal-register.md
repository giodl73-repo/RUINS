# Refusal Register

- Do not treat tooth enamel oxygen values as exact birthplace.
- Do not turn broad Alpine/central-Europe fit into a route map.
- Do not infer Stonehenge builder, king, or priest from isotope evidence.
- Do not collapse grave goods, metalworking, and personal origin.
- Do not mix isotope mobility with DNA kinship or ancestry without a boundary.
- Do not let public Stonehenge memory outrun the sample evidence.
