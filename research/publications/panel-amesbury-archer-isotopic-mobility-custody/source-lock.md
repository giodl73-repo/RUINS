# Source Lock

Locked claims:

- Oxygen isotope analysis of dental enamel indicates the Archer came from a
  colder climate region than Britain today, possibly central Europe.
- Wessex Archaeology later summarizes the likely origin as a considerable
  distance away, probably the Alpine region of central Europe.
- British strontium/oxygen baseline work provides the comparison context that
  makes exact origin claims probabilistic rather than exact.
- Later DNA/family discussion is a separate evidence layer from isotope origin
  inference.

Future-locked:

- Exact birthplace or route.
- Direct Stonehenge builder, king, or metalworker identity claims.
- Kinship/ancestry synthesis.
