# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use ancient fingerprint evidence without turning biometric traces into
exact maker identity or complete labor organization?

## Scope

- Use the Ancestral Pueblo PNAS paper as the primary trigger.
- Use Tell es-Safi/Gath and later ridge-density modelling work as method
  pressure.
- Use clay-shrinkage experiments as the strongest caution source.
- Separate trace, clay, shrinkage, measurement, reference population, model
  uncertainty, demographic category, craft behavior, and public memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No named individual maker claim.
- No certain sex or precise age claim for any single print.
- No complete Chaco gender system or workforce census.
- No settlement-wide labor history without sibling-system synthesis.
