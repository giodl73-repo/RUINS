# Paleoparasitology Gate

| Layer | Latrine custody question |
|---|---|
| Deposit | Is the sample latrine fill, coprolite, pelvic sediment, drain, midden, chamber pot, or mixed deposit? |
| Context | Which site, feature, date range, stratigraphy, reuse, and disturbance history are locked? |
| Sampling | Were concentration, extraction, subsampling, controls, and contamination tracked? |
| Parasite stage | Is evidence egg, cyst, larva, adult remains, DNA, protein, or immunological signal? |
| Host | Is the taxon human-specific, zoonotic, animal-hosted, environmental, or ambiguous? |
| Assignment | Which microscopy, ELISA, shotgun DNA, taxonomic, plant, or animal calls are secure? |
| Infection | Is the claim infection, exposure, environmental contamination, diet passage, or animal proximity? |
| Diet | Are plant and animal DNA treated as components rather than a complete diet? |
| Sanitation | Are latrine use, water, waste management, urban systems, and public health separated? |
| Memory | Are ancient-waste and filth stories kept below source-locked inference? |
