# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use ancient parasite and latrine DNA without turning waste deposits
into disease diagnosis, complete diet, sanitation systems, or movement history?

## Scope

- Use Soe et al. PLOS ONE 2018 as the primary trigger.
- Use paleoparasitology and sedimentary ancient-DNA method papers as pressure.
- Separate deposit, context, sampling, parasite stage, host specificity,
  molecular assignment, infection versus exposure, diet, sanitation, and memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No individual diagnosis from a parasite signal.
- No complete diet from plant or animal DNA.
- No migration route or trade proof from host/parasite distribution alone.
- No public-health or sanitation-system synthesis without sibling ownership.
