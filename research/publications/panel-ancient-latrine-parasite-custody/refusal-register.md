# Refusal Register

- Do not treat a parasite egg as an individual diagnosis.
- Do not treat sedimentary DNA as a complete diet.
- Do not treat host markers as migration routes.
- Do not treat zoonotic parasites as complete animal-management systems.
- Do not treat latrine fill as a full sanitation system.
- Do not treat pathogen markers as public-health history.
- Do not let ancient-waste or filth narratives become social explanation.
