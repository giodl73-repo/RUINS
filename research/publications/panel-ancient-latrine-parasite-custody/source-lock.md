# Source Lock

Locked claims:

- The PLOS ONE paper reports ancient DNA from latrines in Northern Europe and
  the Middle East, dated 500 BC-1700 AD.
- The paper uses parasite egg pre-concentration from ancient latrines and
  deposits followed by shotgun sequencing.
- The paper frames complementary parasite, vertebrate, and plant DNA profiles
  as informative for ancient health, human-animal interactions, and dietary
  components.
- Method-context sources identify latrine sediment, coprolites, and pelvic
  sediment as paleoparasitology materials and name microscopy and ELISA as
  detection methods.
