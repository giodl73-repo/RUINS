# Pathogen Genome Gate

| Gate | Required question |
|---|---|
| Sample custody | Which tooth, pulp, bone, calculus, burial, individual, cemetery, extract, and lab batch are analyzed? |
| Context custody | Is the burial individual, multiple, mass, disturbed, curated, dated, or historically associated? |
| Screening custody | Is the signal shotgun hit, capture positive, qPCR, metagenomic profile, or assembled genome? |
| Authentication custody | Are damage, fragment length, blanks, contamination, mapping quality, and replication recorded? |
| Coverage custody | Is the claim based on low coverage, partial genome, high coverage, SNP panel, or absence? |
| Taxon custody | Is the assignment species, lineage, strain, plasmid, virulence marker, or ambiguous call? |
| Chronology custody | Is the date direct, associated, modelled, molecular-clock, or historically inferred? |
| Epidemiology custody | Are infection, disease, cause of death, outbreak, vector, reservoir, and transmission separated? |
| Memory custody | Are plague-origin, pandemic-certainty, collapse, blame, and public-health analogy contained? |
