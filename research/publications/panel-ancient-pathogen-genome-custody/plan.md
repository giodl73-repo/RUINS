# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS score papers that use ancient pathogen DNA to reconstruct
disease presence, outbreak, transmission, population impact, or plague history?

## Scope

- Ancient pathogen DNA from dental pulp, bone, calculus, skeletal remains, and
  burial contexts.
- Metagenomic screening, capture enrichment, shotgun sequencing, genome
  coverage, SNPs, plasmids, virulence markers, and phylogenies.
- Authentication, contamination, chronology, epidemiological scale, and public
  plague memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No complete disease diagnosis.
- No certain cause of death.
- No exact transmission route, vector, or reservoir.
- No population collapse, migration proof, or historical event attribution.
- No pathogen method benchmark.

## Target Artifact

Promote ancient pathogen genome custody directly to Core with a checklist, gate,
refusal register, sibling-boundary register, and rubric v1.63 cap.
