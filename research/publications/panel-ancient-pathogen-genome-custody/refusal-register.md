# Refusal Register

- Do not treat a pathogen read as disease diagnosis.
- Do not treat a positive genome as cause of death.
- Do not treat a mass burial as proven outbreak.
- Do not treat a virulence gene as transmission route.
- Do not treat plasmid presence as vector certainty.
- Do not treat a phylogenetic branch as migration route.
- Do not treat a molecular clock as exact origin.
- Do not treat a plague headline as population collapse.
