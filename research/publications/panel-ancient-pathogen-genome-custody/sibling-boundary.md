# Sibling Boundary

| Surface | Owner | RUINS boundary |
|---|---|---|
| Remains, burials, cemeteries, samples, datasets, publications, and named outbreaks | CANON | RUINS gates evidence use but does not own entity identity. |
| aDNA authentication, metagenomics, genome assembly, coverage, SNPs, phylogenies, and molecular clocks | GENES | RUINS records custody controls, not method implementation. |
| Disease diagnosis, virulence, pathology, transmission, vectors, reservoirs, and cause of death | Medical systems | RUINS refuses diagnostic closure from sequence data alone. |
| Geography and spread routes | PORTO | RUINS does not own route reconstruction. |
| Population impact, migration, historical event attribution, and pandemic history | MAXIM/LUCIA | RUINS blocks overclaiming and hands synthesis away. |
| Plague and pandemic narratives | Public-history systems | RUINS contains public memory. |
