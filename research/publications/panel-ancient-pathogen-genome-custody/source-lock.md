# Source Lock

## Locked Claims

- Ancient pathogen DNA can be recovered from archaeological human remains.
- Yersinia pestis genomes have been reconstructed from Bronze Age and later
  remains.
- Pathogen genomes can support taxon assignment, strain placement, and
  evolutionary inference under authentication controls.
- Virulence markers and plasmids can inform, but not close, transmission and
  disease-history claims.

## Future-Locked Claims

- Complete disease diagnosis.
- Certain cause of death.
- Proven outbreak from one positive.
- Pandemic origin.
- Exact transmission route, vector, or host reservoir.
- Population collapse or migration proof.
- Immune-selection history.
- Historical event attribution.
- Solved plague story.
