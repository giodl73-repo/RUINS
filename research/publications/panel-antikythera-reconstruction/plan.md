# Plan: Antikythera Reconstruction

## Question

How should RUINS separate surviving mechanism, imaging evidence, mathematical
model, physical replica, and public "ancient computer" memory?

## Deliverables

1. Classify surviving fragments, gear traces, inscriptions, scans, and models.
2. Distinguish observed components from reconstructed gear trains.
3. Treat CT imaging and polynomial-surface imaging as evidence surfaces, not as
   direct ancient parts.
4. Refuse claims that a modern working model is simply "the mechanism."
5. Explain how the device became a symbol of lost ancient technology.

## First Pass Scope

This pass is a working paper. It can use public summaries and major paper
targets, but it should not claim a final reconstruction until source custody and
page anchors are locked.

## Out Of Scope

- Building a gear model.
- Maintaining part identifiers or CAD files.
- Full history of Hellenistic astronomy.
- Claims about the ship's complete cargo or owner.
