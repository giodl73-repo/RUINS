**Owner:** Sibling Boundary Editor
**Status:** publication-scope boundary register
**Date:** 2026-07-04

RUINS owns the partial-survival and reconstruction-boundary problem. It does not
own the whole history of Greek astronomy, scan archives, gear identity canon, or
craft economy.

## Boundary Map

| Surface | RUINS role | Handoff | Do not do here |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ancient Greek astronomy and mathematics | Mention only where it frames the model claim. | MAXIM | Write a history of ancient astronomy. |
| PTM, CT, scan, photo, and 3D model files | Record bounded custody and claim permissions. | MUNDUS / FONTES | Become the data repository. |
| Fragment, gear, dial, and inscription labels | Treat as identity surfaces affecting claim grade. | CANON | Maintain the canonical part registry. |

| Workshop, craft, production, and transmission | Treat as broader hypothesis. | CERES | Reconstruct ancient production economy. |
| Physical replicas and educational models | Treat as modern reconstructions. | RUINS / future model catalog | Treat a replica as the original artifact. |

## Expansion Rule

If a section requires CAD, gear tooth tables, scan files, object inventories,
shipwreck maps, or workshop economics, it should become a handoff note rather
than remain inside this paper.
