# Macroremain Gate

| Gate | Required question |
|---|---|
| Sample custody | Which context, sediment volume, flot, heavy fraction, mesh size, and archive record are analyzed? |
| Recovery custody | Which sampling, flotation, screen, sorting, and lab methods shaped the assemblage? |
| Preservation custody | Is the remain charred, waterlogged, desiccated, mineralized, contaminated, intrusive, or redeposited? |
| Taxon custody | Is the identification family, genus, species, crop, wild taxon, weed, or unknown? |
| Taphonomy custody | Was burning, dumping, hearth rake-out, dung fuel, storage loss, bioturbation, or mixing tested? |
| Quantification custody | Are count, weight, density, ubiquity, ratio, absence, and sample volume separate? |
| Processing custody | Are harvest, threshing, winnowing, sieving, dehusking, storage, cooking, and discard separated? |
| Scale custody | Is the inference sample, feature, room, household, settlement, region, or economy scale? |
| Memory custody | Are first-farming, ancient meal, granary, famine, and ritual stories contained? |
