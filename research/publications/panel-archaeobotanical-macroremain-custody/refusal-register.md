# Refusal Register

- Do not treat a charred seed as exact crop inventory.
- Do not treat a flotation count as complete diet.
- Do not treat a chaff ratio as final crop-processing stage.
- Do not treat a weed seed as field ecology.
- Do not treat a storage pit seed cache as full storage system.
- Do not treat an immature grain ratio as harvest calendar.
- Do not treat seed absence as crop absence.
- Do not treat a first-farming headline as domestication proof.
