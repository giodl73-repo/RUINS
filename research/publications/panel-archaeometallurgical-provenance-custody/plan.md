# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS score papers that use slag, metal composition, or lead isotopes
to reconstruct ore source, production, exchange, or technology history?

## Scope

- Slag, slag inclusions, ores, metal artefacts, furnace remains, crucibles,
  tuyeres, prills, and corrosion layers.
- Lead isotopes, trace elements, XRF/pXRF, LA-ICP-MS, SEM-EDS, XRD,
  metallography, microstructure, and compositional databases.
- Provenance probability, recycling/mixing, production stage, exchange
  inference, and public ancient-industry memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact mine attribution.
- No complete trade-route reconstruction.
- No full smelting recipe or workshop biography.
- No political economy or ethnic/craft identity claim.
- No method benchmark or database construction.

## Target Artifact

Promote archaeometallurgical provenance custody directly to Core with a
checklist, gate, refusal register, sibling-boundary register, and rubric v1.61
cap.
