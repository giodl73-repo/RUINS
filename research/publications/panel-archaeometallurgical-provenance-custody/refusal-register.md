# Refusal Register

- Do not treat a lead isotope ratio as an exact mine.
- Do not treat slag chemistry as a complete smelting recipe.
- Do not treat a slag inclusion as a direct workshop.
- Do not treat an XRF cluster as a trade route.
- Do not treat an ore database match as solved provenance.
- Do not treat furnace residue as production scale.
- Do not treat alloy composition as ethnic or craft identity.
- Do not treat a metal circulation map as political economy.
