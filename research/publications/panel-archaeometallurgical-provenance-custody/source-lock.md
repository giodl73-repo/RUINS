# Source Lock

## Locked Claims

- Lead-isotope analysis is a long-used method for metal provenance studies.
- Isotope overlap and database scope limit exact source attribution.
- Slag inclusions and slag chemistry can support production-provenance
  hypotheses but require caution.
- Archaeometallurgical studies combine chemical, mineralogical, microscopic, and
  contextual evidence to reconstruct production processes.
