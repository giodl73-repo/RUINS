# Plan: Archimedes Palimpsest Overwrite Custody

**Owner:** ASHLAR
**Status:** publication-ready
**Date:** 2026-07-06

## Problem

The Archimedes Palimpsest is a spectacular recovery story, but RUINS cannot let
"recovered by imaging" become "the original text is fully restored." The object
is a thirteenth-century prayer book made from earlier manuscripts, including
erased Archimedes text. It also has missing leaves, mold, forged paintings,
conservation interventions, multispectral imaging, XRF imaging, processed
images, scholarly readings, and public data release.

## RUINS Claim

Palimpsest overwrite custody is required when erased undertext survives through
an overwritten manuscript. Undertext, overtext, material loss, conservation,
imaging modality, image processing, scholar reading, transcription, and digital
publication are different evidence layers.
