# Refusal Register

- Do not call a modern cooked dish the exact ancient taste.
- Do not treat translation as a complete recipe.
- Do not infer precise quantities, timing, temperature, vessels, or technique
  without separate custody.
- Do not equate modern ingredients with ancient terms without a gate.
- Do not treat a public recipe page as safe historical instruction.
- Do not use oldest-recipe memory as proof of complete culinary practice.
- Do not expand into Mesopotamian food history beyond current source lock.
