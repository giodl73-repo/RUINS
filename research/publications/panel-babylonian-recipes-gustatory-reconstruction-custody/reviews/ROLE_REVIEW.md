# Role Review: Babylonian Recipes Gustatory Reconstruction Custody

**Paper:** panel-babylonian-recipes-gustatory-reconstruction-custody
**Rubric:** v1.30
**Review date:** 2026-07-07

## Fragment Custodian

The paper separates tablet witness, text, translation, terse instructions,
ingredient substitution, experimental cooking, public recipe access, and
oldest-recipe memory. Exact editions remain future-locked.

## Absence Historian

The missing object is original taste and kitchen process: quantities, timing,
heat, vessels, ingredient equivalence, serving context, and palate. The paper
does not overread public recipes as ancient meals.

## Reconstruction Skeptic

Refusals block exact taste, complete recipe, translation equivalence, ingredient
equivalence, safety claims, and direct ancient kitchen access.

## Cultural Memory Reader

"Oldest recipe" and cooking-from-tablets memory are contained as public access
and afterlife, not proof of exact ancient food.

## Sibling Boundary Editor

Boundaries are clear: FONTES/MUNDUS gets text, CANON gets tablets, PORTO gets
place, RITE gets context, CERES gets food science, MAXIM/LUCIA gets history,
experience systems get videos/recipes, and safety systems get guidance.

## Paper Editor

The package has a plan, paper, source map, source lock, page-anchor audit, gate,
claim audit, refusal register, memory register, boundary register, and scorecard.

## Safety Editor

No cooking instructions, safety claims, or consumption advice are made beyond
describing public access layers.

## Decision

Promote `ruins-capability:gustatory-reconstruction-custody` to Core. Add
`docs/gustatory-reconstruction-checklist.md` and rubric v1.30 cap.
