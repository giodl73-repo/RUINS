# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS score a destroyed monument when the most public surviving
evidence is an empty niche, fragments, stabilization work, and reconstruction
debate?

## Scope

- Bamiyan World Heritage property and Danger-list frame.
- Empty niches and surviving archaeological remains.
- UNESCO safeguarding, stabilization, and demining claims.
- 2011 expert recommendations on reconstruction and empty-niche testimony.
- 2021 UNESCO memory and intentional-destruction framing.

## Out Of Scope

- Full Buddhist iconography.
- Exact fragment inventory.
- Engineering instructions for stabilization or reconstruction.
- Afghan political history beyond source-scoped context.
- Legal analysis beyond intentional-destruction memory.

## Deliverables

- Publication draft.
- Monumental-void gate.
- Source lock and page-anchor audit.
- Claim audit and refusal register.
- Role review and scorecard.
- Candidate-to-active capability promotion.
