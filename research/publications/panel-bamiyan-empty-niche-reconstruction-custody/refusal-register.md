# Refusal Register

RUINS refuses these current-scope claims:

1. The empty niches are the original Buddhas.
2. The fragments support complete reconstruction without separate inventory and
   technical custody.
3. The western niche testimony settles all future recovery policy.
4. Partial eastern reassembly is already approved as a final plan.
5. Stabilization or demining means conservation is complete.
6. Global shock or intentional-destruction memory restores the lost monument.
7. Bamiyan can be reduced to the 2001 destruction event.
8. The paper can settle Buddhist meaning, Afghan history, legal accountability,
   or community consensus.
