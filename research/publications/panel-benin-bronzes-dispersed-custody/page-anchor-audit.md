## Result

Current claims have public anchors. Remaining limitations are future locks:

1. no complete inventory of all Benin objects,
2. no legal-title or restitution-policy adjudication,
3. no object-level provenance beyond named source examples,
4. no exact ritual, dynastic, or named-owner claim without object custody,
5. no treating digital records, loans, legal transfer, and physical return as
   interchangeable.
