## RUINS Scope

RUINS owns the evidence-boundary problem: how surviving objects, removal,
dispersal, provenance, current holding, legal transfer, physical return, digital
record, and cultural memory should be separated.

## Drift Controls

1. Do not turn this paper into a full Benin history.
2. Do not create an all-object inventory.
3. Do not adjudicate legal title or final custody disputes.
4. Do not treat digital records, loans, returns, and display as interchangeable.
