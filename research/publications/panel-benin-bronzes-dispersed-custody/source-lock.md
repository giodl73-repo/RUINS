# Source Lock: Benin Bronzes Dispersed Custody

**Owner:** PALIMPSEST
**Status:** page-audited source lock
**Date:** 2026-07-05

This source lock permits source-scoped claims about the Benin Bronzes as a
dispersed object corpus. It does not authorize legal adjudication, object-level
provenance for every object, exact ritual use, complete inventory, or final
restitution status.

Current paper claims are anchored in
[`page-anchor-audit.md`](page-anchor-audit.md).

## Paper Use Rules

1. Treat each object's provenance as object-specific.
2. Separate current holding, legal title, deaccession, physical return, loan,
   display, and digital record.
3. Treat Digital Benin as digital reconnection and data custody, not physical
   restitution.
4. Do not decide legal or political custody disputes.
5. Do not infer original ritual use or named owner without object-level custody.
