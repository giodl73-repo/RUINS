# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use chewed birch-pitch biomolecular evidence without turning one
masticated object into a complete person, last meal, diagnosis, or lifestyle?

## Scope

- Use Jensen et al. Nature Communications 2019 as the primary source.
- Use PubMed and DTU records for metadata confirmation.
- Use the Huseby Klev Mesolithic chewed-pitch metagenomic paper as pressure for
  oral-health and microbiome overclaims.
- Separate object, chewing, resin preservation, ancient genome, oral microbiome,
  food DNA, pathogen signal, pitch function, and public memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact last meal.
- No disease diagnosis from microbial or viral DNA alone.
- No direct medical-treatment claim from pitch chewing.
- No complete Stone Age lifestyle or population-history synthesis.
