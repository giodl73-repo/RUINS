# Page Audit: Cahokia Without Collapse Theater

**Owner:** ERRATA
**Status:** current-claim page audit
**Date:** 2026-07-04

This audit anchors the paper's current source-locked claims to public page,
abstract, PDF, or line locations. It does not authorize detailed feature-level
claims about Mound 72, burials, Woodhenge construction, palisade construction,
or descendant-community mapping.

## Result

The current source-locked claims have public anchors. The remaining limitations
are scope limits rather than publication blockers:

1. no feature-level Mound 72, burial, Woodhenge, or palisade construction claim
   without separate audit,
2. no exact demographic reconstruction beyond source-labeled estimates and
   watershed-specific proxy claims,
3. no single-cause abandonment explanation,
4. no "vanished people" language,
5. no conversion of heritage interpretation into excavation evidence.
