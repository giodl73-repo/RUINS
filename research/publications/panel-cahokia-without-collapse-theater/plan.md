# Plan: Cahokia Without Collapse Theater

## Question

What can RUINS say about Cahokia's abandonment and transformation without
turning it into a theatrical "collapse" story?

## Deliverables

1. Separate surviving earthworks, excavated features, environmental proxies,
   and modern public interpretation.
2. Treat population peak, urban scale, and abandonment as source-specific
   claims.
3. Compare environmental, social, political, and demographic hypotheses without
   collapsing them into one cause.
4. Refuse "lost civilization vanished" framing where it erases Indigenous
   continuity or evidence limits.
5. Explain how Cahokia became a public symbol of North American urban antiquity.

## Current Scope

This pass is a stable working paper. It source-locks major public-site values,
environmental hypotheses, wood-overuse critique, and post-abandonment
continuity framing. It does not yet include page-level anchors for exact
numeric, proxy, or stratigraphic claims.

## Out Of Scope

- Site guide or tourism writing.
- Archaeological excavation advice.
- Full Mississippian history.
- Object-level inventory of Mound 72, Monks Mound, Woodhenge, or surrounding
  sites.
