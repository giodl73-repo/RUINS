## RUINS Scope

RUINS owns the absence discipline: what survives, what is missing, what cannot
be responsibly reconstructed, and which modern stories must be treated as
afterlife rather than evidence.

## Drift Controls

1. Do not turn this paper into a site guide.
2. Do not build a complete Mississippian encyclopedia here.
3. Do not inventory burials or sensitive feature details without a separate
   scoped review.
4. Do not map descendant communities or identities without a MAXIM-led plan.
