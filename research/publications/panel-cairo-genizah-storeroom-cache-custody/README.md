# Cairo Genizah Storeroom Cache Custody

Status: publication-ready
Track: Evidence and Absence
Rubric: v1.17
Score: 46/50

The Cairo Genizah is the long-use storeroom countercase to Dunhuang's sealed
cave cache. It preserves sacred and everyday manuscript fragments from the
Ben Ezra Synagogue genizah and related collecting histories, but its abundance,
dispersal, cataloguing, and digital reaggregation must not become a complete
community archive or whole Mediterranean social history.
