# Source Lock: Cairo Genizah

## Locked For Current Claims

| Source | Locked claim |
|---|---|
| Cambridge Digital Library, `Cairo Genizah` | The Genizah was a sacred storeroom where manuscripts were stored by the community of Fustat. |
| Cambridge University Library, Taylor-Schechter Genizah Research Unit | The Taylor-Schechter Cairo Genizah Collection at Cambridge is the largest and most important single collection of medieval Jewish manuscripts. |
| Friedberg Genizah Project / Friedberg Jewish Manuscript Society | The project preserves images of Genizah fragments with identifications, catalogue records, bibliographic data, and related information. |
| Princeton Digital PUL, Friedberg Genizah Project | The project aggregates digital images and metadata from many libraries and includes catalog entries, bibliographic records, identifications, and transcriptions. |

This paper is publication-ready because it makes custody and representativeness
claims while refusing exact textual and broad historical synthesis claims.
