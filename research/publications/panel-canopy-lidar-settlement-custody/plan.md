# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS score papers that use airborne LiDAR to turn hidden terrain
signals into settlement, urbanism, land-use, population, or lost-city claims?

## Scope

- Airborne LiDAR and canopy-filtered terrain models.
- Settlement candidates, roads, causeways, terraces, field systems, reservoirs,
  canals, and urban landscapes.
- Ground-truthing, error rates, chronology, function, population modelling, and
  public discovery memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No complete Maya, Angkor, Amazonian, or wetland-field history.
- No model implementation benchmark.
- No final population number.
- No route, polity, or collapse explanation.
- No conservation prioritization without separate governance evidence.

## Target Artifact

Promote canopy LiDAR settlement custody directly to Core with a checklist,
gate, refusal register, sibling-boundary register, and rubric v1.59 cap.
