# Refusal Register

- Do not treat a point cloud as a complete settlement inventory.
- Do not treat a hillshade as excavation-equivalent confirmation.
- Do not treat a feature candidate as a confirmed building.
- Do not treat a road trace as a complete political network.
- Do not treat a structure count as exact population.
- Do not treat a survey polygon as the whole-region map.
- Do not treat environmental LiDAR as archaeological coverage.
- Do not treat a lost-city image as rediscovered civilization.
