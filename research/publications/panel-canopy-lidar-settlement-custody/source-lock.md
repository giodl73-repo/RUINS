# Source Lock

## Locked Claims

- Airborne LiDAR can produce high-resolution terrain models below vegetation.
- LiDAR can reveal candidate archaeological features not visible in routine
  ground survey.
- Maya, Angkor, Amazonian, and wetland-field papers use LiDAR to revise
  settlement and landscape interpretations.
- Some LiDAR papers pair remote-sensing interpretation with ground truthing,
  excavation, surface collection, or multiproxy evidence.

## Future-Locked Claims

- Complete settlement inventory.
- Exact population count.
- Complete political or urban organization.
- Full road or causeway network.
- Final chronology for visible features.
- Excavation-equivalent confirmation.
- Environmental determinism or solved collapse.
- Conservation-complete map.
