# Plan

## Question

Can astronomical-alignment custody handle a digital restoration of a light and
shadow site whose present condition no longer preserves the original markings?

## Scope

- Use Sun Dagger Explorer, UMBC, UCAR, and digital-restoration summaries.
- Separate slabs, spirals, scanning, model, solar/lunar cycles, observation,
  calendar claim, ritual meaning, Indigenous/governance boundary, and memory.
- Promote the capability to Core with rubric v1.33 and a checklist.

## Out Of Scope

- No site-location, access, or visitation guidance.
- No final Pueblo calendrical or ritual interpretation.
- No implementation of the digital model.
