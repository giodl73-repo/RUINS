# Source Lock

Locked claims:

- The Sun Dagger Explorer is an interactive computer model of a Chaco calendar
  site.
- Records describe slabs casting light/shadow markings on spiral petroglyphs.
- The model uses scanning/photogrammetry and supports time/cycle exploration.
- Public summaries report slab movement and changed light markings after
  rediscovery.
