# Claim Audit

| Claim | Grade | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Cheddar Man is an ancient-genomics custody case. | Strong | The case combines ancient DNA, phenotype prediction, reconstruction, and public identity memory. |
| Genome-wide data can support population-affinity claims. | Strong | The primary paper frames Mesolithic and Neolithic population relationships. |
| DNA predicts exact face and skin tone. | Refused | Phenotype markers and reconstruction choices are probabilistic and mediated. |
| Western hunter-gatherer affinity equals modern race. | Refused | Ancient population affinity is not a modern racial category. |
| Facial reconstruction is useful public communication. | Moderate | Useful if labelled as reconstruction, not recovered person. |
| Cheddar Man is the national ancestor of modern Britons. | Refused | Direct continuity and identity require separate evidence. |
