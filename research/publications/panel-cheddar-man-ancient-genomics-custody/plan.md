# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS score ancient DNA reconstructions without turning genome data
into identity, race, or complete biography?

## Scope

- Use Natural History Museum, UCL, and Nature Ecology & Evolution / PMC records
  as source anchors.
- Separate sample, contamination/coverage, reference population, ancestry model,
  phenotype prediction, facial reconstruction, population history, and memory.
- Activate ancient genomics reconstruction custody.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact face or skin-tone certainty.
- No modern racial or national identity claim.
- No full Mesolithic/Neolithic population synthesis.
