## Evidence Surfaces

| Surface | Evidence condition | RUINS use |
|---|---|---|
| Physical fragments | direct material | Establish survival and material custody. |
| Cave and discovery records | reported / archaeological context | Track provenance and discovery limitations. |
| Plate photographs and digital images | mediated material record | Anchor visual claims without substituting for fragments. |
| DJD and other scholarly editions | edited textual custody | Track transcription, numbering, joins, and readings. |
| Translations | interpretation | Separate readable English from surviving ancient text. |
| Fragment joins and lacuna reconstructions | scholarly reconstruction | Mark supplied text and confidence. |
| Authentication reports | material analysis / custody dispute | Bound modern-market fragment claims. |
| Museum/public narratives | cultural memory | Track scripture, secrecy, treasure, and heritage afterlife. |
