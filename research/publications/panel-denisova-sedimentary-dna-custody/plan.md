# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use sedimentary ancient DNA without turning cave dirt into occupants,
encounters, artefact makers, or complete occupation history?

## Scope

- Use the 2021 Denisova Cave Nature paper as primary trigger.
- Use the 2017 Science sediment DNA paper as method pressure.
- Use microstratigraphic hotspot work as preservation pressure.
- Separate sample, stratigraphy, deposition, spatial grid, DNA source,
  authentication, assignment, association, time inference, absence, and memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact individual presence from a sediment signal.
- No continuous occupation from layer presence.
- No direct Neanderthal/Denisovan encounter from co-occurrence alone.
- No artefact-maker identity or migration route without sibling-owned synthesis.
