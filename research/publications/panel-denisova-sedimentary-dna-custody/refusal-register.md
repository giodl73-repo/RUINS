# Refusal Register

- Do not treat sediment DNA as exact individual presence.
- Do not treat layer signal as continuous occupation.
- Do not infer direct species encounter from co-occurrence alone.
- Do not identify artefact makers from sediment DNA without independent association.
- Do not turn faunal turnover into a migration route.
- Do not treat missing DNA as proof of absence.
- Do not let no-bones-needed headlines become complete site history.
