# Sedimentary DNA Gate

| Layer | Sedimentary-DNA custody question |
|---|---|
| Sample | Which sediment sample, block, grid point, chamber, layer, or microstratigraphic unit produced the DNA? |
| Stratigraphy | What date, layer, disturbance, mixing, redeposition, and association controls are locked? |
| Deposition | Is DNA from sediment matrix, microscopic bone, feces, body fluid, hair, skin, water movement, or unknown source? |
| Spatial grid | Is the claim point sample, grid pattern, layer average, chamber history, or site-wide model? |
| DNA source | Is the signal mtDNA, nuclear DNA, faunal DNA, microbial DNA, environmental DNA, or mixed? |
| Authentication | Are damage, fragment length, contamination, extraction blank, and replication separated? |
| Assignment | Is the call species, lineage, population, individual, hybrid relation, or unresolved taxon? |
| Association | Are DNA, artefacts, remains, fauna, dates, and sediment processes independently linked? |
| Time | Is the claim presence, turnover, repeated presence, continuous occupation, overlap, or absence? |
| Memory | Are no-bones-needed and species-meeting stories held below source-locked inference? |
