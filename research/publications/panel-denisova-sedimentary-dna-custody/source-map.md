# Source Map

| Source | Use |
|---|---|
| Zavala et al., "Pleistocene sediment DNA reveals hominin and faunal turnovers at Denisova Cave", Nature, https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-021-03675-0 | Primary source for 728 grid-collected sediment samples, all three chambers, hominin/faunal DNA, and turnover/occupation-history framing. |
| PubMed record, https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/34163072/ | Metadata and abstract confirmation. |
| Max Planck press page, https://www.mpg.de/17061997/0618-evan-pleistocene-sediment-dna-from-denisova-cave-150495-x | Context source for largest sediment-DNA study, 300,000-year occupation framing, Neanderthal and Denisovan DNA detection. |
| Slon et al., "Neandertal and Denisovan DNA from Pleistocene sediments", Science, https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/science.aam9695 | Method pressure for sediment as source of ancient mammalian and hominin DNA even without hominin remains. |
| "Microstratigraphic preservation of ancient faunal and hominin DNA in Pleistocene cave sediments", PNAS, https://www.pnas.org/doi/10.1073/pnas.2113666118 | Pressure source for DNA hotspots and microscopic bone/fecal particles. |
| Max Planck, "DNA in archaeological sediments", https://www.mpg.de/18092942/dna-in-archaeological-sediments | Public-method context for DNA concentration in microscopic particles of bone or feces. |
