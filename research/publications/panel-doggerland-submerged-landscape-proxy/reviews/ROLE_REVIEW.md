# Role Review: Doggerland Submerged Landscape Proxy

## Fragment Custodian

Pass. The paper separates bathymetry, seismic, cores, artefacts, faunal
evidence, DNA, models, and public reconstructions instead of treating them as a
single recovered landscape.

## Absence Historian

Pass. The loss is processual: postglacial change, inundation, sediment movement,
and later seabed disturbance are not collapsed into one catastrophe.

## Reconstruction Skeptic

Pass. The refusal register blocks settlement maps, precise routes, named
cultures, ancestry claims, and lost-civilization framing.

## Cultural Memory Reader

Pass. "Lost world" language is handled as public afterlife and not as original
evidence.

## Sibling Boundary Editor

Pass. Geography, sediment science, DNA, population history, named cultures, and
general prehistory are handed to sibling systems.

## ASHLAR Decision

Publication-ready. Promote `ruins-capability:submerged-landscape-proxy-custody`
from Candidate to Active. Do not promote to Core until an unlike submerged case
tests the gate against in-situ settlement evidence.
