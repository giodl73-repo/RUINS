# Refusal Register

- Do not call the rebuilt Frauenkirche complete original fabric.
- Do not treat reused stones as object-equivalence for the whole monument.
- Do not convert historical plans, floor plans, or photographs into exact
  interior recovery.
- Do not treat photogrammetry or a computer-aided model as direct access to the
  lost church.
- Do not flatten structural correction into original Baroque engineering.
- Do not use peace, reconciliation, or European-unity memory as proof of total
  heritage recovery.
- Do not make Dresden a Warsaw-equivalent reconstruction doctrine.
