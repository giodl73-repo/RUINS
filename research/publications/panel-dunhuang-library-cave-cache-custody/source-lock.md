# Source Lock: Dunhuang Library Cave

## Locked For Current Claims

| Source | Locked claim |
|---|---|
| IDP, `Discovering Cave 17` | Cave 17 was sealed for centuries and contained over 50,000 manuscripts, paintings, printed documents, textiles, and other objects dating from the 4th to 11th centuries. |
| IDP, `Discovering Cave 17` | Wang Yuanlu discovered the hidden chamber, became custodian/keyholder, and later sold or distributed bundles to foreign scholars and archaeologists including Stein. |
| Terzi and Whitfield 2024 | The original purpose of the cache is unsettled, and Stein documentation can help reconstruct some bundle contents but not the original order of the cave itself. |
| IDP homepage | IDP is an international collaboration bringing together online collections from the Eastern Silk Roads. |
| BnF IDP page | IDP catalogues, digitizes, and virtually brings together scattered manuscripts, objects, and works of art from Dunhuang-related sites. |
