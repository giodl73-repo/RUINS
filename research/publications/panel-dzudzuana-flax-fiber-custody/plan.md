# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use microscopic fiber evidence without turning partial perishable
traces into complete textiles, garments, cordage systems, or craft history?

## Scope

- Use the Dzudzuana Science paper and PubMed record as the primary trigger.
- Use open repository/PDF copies and site-context summaries as pressure sources.
- Separate sample, layer, fiber identity, preservation, contamination,
  modification, color, technique, object inference, activity scale, and memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact garment, woven textile, or complete basket.
- No complete dyeing technology from color alone.
- No craft specialization, trade route, or fashion history.
- No oldest-clothing claim without direct object custody.
