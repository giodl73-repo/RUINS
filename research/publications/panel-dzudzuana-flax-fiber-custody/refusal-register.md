# Refusal Register

- Do not treat microscopic fibers as complete textiles.
- Do not treat twist as a whole cordage system.
- Do not treat colored fibers as complete dyeing technology.
- Do not treat cut ends as complete sewing practice.
- Do not infer exact garments, woven cloth, or full baskets.
- Do not turn fiber traces into craft specialization or trade networks.
- Do not let oldest-textile memory become oldest-clothing history.
