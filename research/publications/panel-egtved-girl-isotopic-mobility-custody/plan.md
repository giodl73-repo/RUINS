# Plan

How should RUINS score isotope-based mobility reconstructions without turning
chemical signals into biography?

## Scope

- Use later Aarhus and public summaries as baseline-dispute and memory anchors.
- Separate samples, tissue windows, baselines, contamination, mobility, identity,
  textile provenance, and public origin stories.

## Out Of Scope
