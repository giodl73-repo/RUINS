method. It is not a bibliography yet.

## Seed Evidence Surfaces

| Case | Direct material | Direct textual | Copied / reported | Reconstruction | Known absence |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Indus script | inscribed seals, tablets, pottery marks, short sign sequences | none securely deciphered | excavation reports, sign lists, corpus catalogs | proposed sign systems and language mappings | no accepted decipherment, limited bilingual evidence |
| Antikythera mechanism | recovered fragments, gear remains, inscriptions | inscriptions on fragments | excavation history, early descriptions, imaging reports | gear-train models, functional reconstructions, replicas | missing gears, incomplete casing, uncertain full operation |
| Library of Alexandria | uncertain direct material survival for the institution as popularly imagined | ancient references to libraries, scholars, and collections | later accounts of destruction and loss | modern institutional reconstructions and popular narratives | collection extent, exact holdings, single loss event |
| Cahokia | earthworks, settlement remains, artifacts, environmental traces | no local textual record | later archaeology, colonial-era indirect records, oral and regional memory contexts | settlement models, population estimates, collapse scenarios | internal political record, full social chronology |
| Roman concrete | surviving structures and material samples | ancient technical references | later interpretations, recipe discussions | material-science reconstructions and experimental mixes | exact workshop practice, continuity gaps, regional variation |
