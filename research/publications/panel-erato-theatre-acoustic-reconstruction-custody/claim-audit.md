# Claim Audit

| Claim | Grade | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| ERATO is an acoustic-space reconstruction custody case. | Strong | Sources anchor models, calibration, archaeological completion, virtual reconstruction, and auralization. |
| Virtual reconstructions are direct ancient hearing. | Refused | Models, measurements, assumptions, playback, and reconstructed music mediate the output. |
| ERATO helps compare theatres and odea. | Strong | Public abstracts explicitly frame this comparison. |
| Reconstructed instruments recover original performance. | Refused | They are modern reconstruction choices. |
| Theatre acoustics prove design intention. | Refused | Acoustic quality does not settle intention or function. |
| Auralizations are useful access layers. | Moderate | Useful when labelled as modelled output, not ancient event. |
