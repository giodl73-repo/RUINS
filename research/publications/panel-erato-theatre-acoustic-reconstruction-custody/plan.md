# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS grade a lost theatre sound environment reconstructed through
computer models, archaeological completion, calibration, and auralization?

## Evidence Surfaces

- Present-state theatre and odeon ruins.
- Computer models.
- Acoustical measurements for calibration.
- Archaeological completion of missing geometry.
- Virtual acoustic reconstructions.
- Reconstructed instruments and short music recordings.
- Open theatre versus roofed odeon comparison.
- Public "revival" and listening memory.

## Exclusions

- Full theatre history.
- Final performance practice.
- Exact audience experience.
- Complete instrument reconstruction.
- Software implementation.
- Heritage presentation design.

## Promotion Target

Use ERATO as the unlike second test for
`ruins-capability:acoustic-space-reconstruction-custody`, then promote the
capability to Core with a cross-case checklist and rubric v1.31 cap.
