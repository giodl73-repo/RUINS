# Role Review: ERATO Theatre Acoustic Reconstruction Custody

**Paper:** panel-erato-theatre-acoustic-reconstruction-custody
**Rubric:** v1.31
**Review date:** 2026-07-07

## Fragment Custodian

The paper separates present ruin, computer model, calibration, archaeological
completion, virtual reconstruction, reconstructed music, listening demo, and
public memory. Exact model files and parameters remain future-locked.

## Absence Historian

The missing condition is original performance environment and audience
experience. The paper distinguishes modelled acoustics from ancient events.

## Reconstruction Skeptic

Refusals block direct hearing, original performance, design intention, uniform
theatre acoustics, calibration overreach, and demo equivalence.

## Cultural Memory Reader

"Excellent acoustics" and "revival" are handled as reception and access layers,
not as proof that every ancient theatre sounded the same or served one function.

## Sibling Boundary Editor

Boundaries are clear: PORTO gets sites, CANON gets identities, CERES gets
acoustics and software, RITE gets performance, MAXIM/LUCIA gets history,
experience systems get demos, and heritage systems get interpretation.

## Paper Editor

The package has a plan, paper, source map, source lock, page-anchor audit, gate,
claim audit, refusal register, memory register, boundary register, and scorecard.

## Safety Editor

No site access, conservation, or intervention instructions.

## Decision

Promote `ruins-capability:acoustic-space-reconstruction-custody` to Core. Add
`docs/acoustic-space-reconstruction-checklist.md` and rubric v1.31 cap.
