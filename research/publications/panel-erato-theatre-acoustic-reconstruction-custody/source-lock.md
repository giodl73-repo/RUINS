# Source Lock

Current publication claims are locked to DTU Orbit and the Rindel ERATO paper.

## Locked Claims

- ERATO investigated open-air theatres and roofed odea.
- The method used computer models of spaces as they exist today.
- Acoustic data were adjusted by comparison with measurements from preserved
  examples such as Aspendos and Jerash.
- Models were completed according to archaeological information to create
  virtual reconstructions.
- The work simulated acoustic qualities of ancient performance spaces.
- Musical instruments and short pieces of music were reconstructed and recorded.
