# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS score papers that use geophysical survey to turn subsurface
signals into buried buildings, city plans, sacred landscapes, or complete
site-function claims?

## Scope

- GPR, magnetometry, resistivity, EMI, conductivity, magnetic susceptibility,
  and gradiometry.
- Radar time slices, amplitude/depth maps, anomaly clusters, integrated maps,
  motorized arrays, and survey grids.
- Feature candidates, ground truthing, chronology, function, plan synthesis,
  and public nondestructive-discovery memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No complete Roman urban history.
- No final Stonehenge sacred-landscape interpretation.
- No excavation-equivalent claim from survey imagery.
- No instrument-performance benchmark.
- No preservation or conservation status without separate evidence.

## Target Artifact

Promote geophysical subsurface survey custody directly to Core with a checklist,
gate, refusal register, sibling-boundary register, and rubric v1.60 cap.
