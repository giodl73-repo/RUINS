# Refusal Register

- Do not treat a GPR time slice as a confirmed building.
- Do not treat a magnetometry plot as a complete street grid.
- Do not treat an anomaly cluster as a certain tomb or ritual feature.
- Do not treat a depth slice as final chronology.
- Do not treat an interpolated grid as an exact wall line.
- Do not treat nondestructive survey as excavation-equivalent proof.
- Do not treat an integrated map as the whole city.
- Do not treat a hidden-monument image as solved sacred landscape.
