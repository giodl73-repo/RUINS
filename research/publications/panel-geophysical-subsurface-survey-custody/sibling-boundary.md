# Sibling Boundary

| Surface | Owner | RUINS boundary |
|---|---|---|
| Sites, features, survey grids, datasets, publications, and named monuments | CANON | RUINS gates evidence use but does not own entity identity. |
| Instrument physics, soil contrast, processing, georeferencing, depth modelling, interference, and preservation science | CERES | RUINS records custody controls, not geophysical method implementation. |
| Landscape geography | PORTO | RUINS does not own regional geography. |
| Urbanism, site function, chronology, and history synthesis | MAXIM/LUCIA | RUINS blocks overclaiming and hands synthesis away. |
| Ritual or sacred-landscape interpretation | RITE | RUINS only audits evidence custody. |
| City-without-digging and hidden-monument narratives | Public-history systems | RUINS contains public memory. |
