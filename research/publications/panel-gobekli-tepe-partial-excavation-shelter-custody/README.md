# Gobekli Tepe Partial Excavation Shelter Custody

Status: publication-ready
Track: Evidence and Absence
Rubric: v1.20
Score: 45/50

Gobekli Tepe is a RUINS case for partially excavated, sheltered public
knowledge. The visitor sees exposed monumental buildings and conservation
infrastructure, but the evidence also includes unexcavated deposits, protective
shelters, visitor routes, conservation interventions, and changing scholarly
interpretation.
