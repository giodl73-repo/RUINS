|---|---|---|

## Publication Rules

1. Put Queen of Sheba, Biblical, Phoenician, Arab, or other non-African origin
   language in denial/memory context.
2. Do not describe the site as mysterious when evidence and consensus are the
   relevant issues.
3. Separate national symbolism from medieval identity.
4. Treat local ritual and sacred claims as living memory requiring source
   posture.
