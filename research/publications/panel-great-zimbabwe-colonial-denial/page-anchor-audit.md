# Page Anchor Audit: Great Zimbabwe Colonial Denial

**Owner:** PALIMPSEST / ERRATA
**Status:** current-scope page audit
**Date:** 2026-07-04

This audit locks the page or line anchors that support the current paper claims.
It does not authorize feature-level excavation claims, conservation advice,
full identity-continuity claims, or a final population value.
