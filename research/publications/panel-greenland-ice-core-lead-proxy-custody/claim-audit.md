# Claim Audit

| Claim | Grade | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Greenland ice-core lead is a RUINS proxy-custody case. | Strong | The case reconstructs human activity from distant environmental deposition. |
| Ice-core lead can support bounded pollution and emissions-proxy claims. | Strong | This is the source claim under chronology, deposition, and model controls. |
| Ice-core lead is exact mining output. | Refused | Deposition and emissions conversion are modelled proxy steps. |
| A pollution curve is a precise economic index. | Refused | Economic interpretation requires independent historical and archaeological custody. |
| A lead drop proves one war or plague. | Refused | Chronological alignment is not event causation. |
| Atmospheric lead is population exposure or IQ loss. | Refused | Exposure and health effects require separate medical modelling. |
| Roman pollution explains civilizational decline. | Refused | Decline narratives exceed the proxy evidence. |
