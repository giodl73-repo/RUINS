# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use ice-core pollution records without turning environmental proxy
curves into exact economy, event, health, or decline narratives?

## Scope

- Use the 2018 PNAS Greenland lead pollution paper as primary trigger.
- Use Arctic medieval lead-pollution and Roman public-health papers as method
  and memory pressure.
- Separate core, chronology, contaminant, deposition, source, transport,
  conversion, event alignment, exposure model, scale, uncertainty, and memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact mining output or precise economic index.
- No single-event proof for war, plague, policy, or expansion.
- No population exposure or IQ loss without medical/exposure-model custody.
- No civilizational decline or complete industrial history.
