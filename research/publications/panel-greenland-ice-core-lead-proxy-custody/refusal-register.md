# Refusal Register

- Do not treat ice-core lead as exact mining output.
- Do not treat deposition flux as a precise economic index.
- Do not infer single-event proof from war, plague, or policy alignment.
- Do not treat transport modelling as complete source certainty.
- Do not turn emissions estimates into population exposure without exposure custody.
- Do not turn atmospheric lead into IQ loss without medical custody.
- Do not let Rome-pollution headlines become civilizational decline.
