## Memory Surfaces

| Memory surface | What it does | RUINS handling |
|---|---|---|
| Lost library | Turns a damaged archive into a symbol of missing antiquity. | Afterlife evidence, not content proof. |
| AI miracle | Makes recovery feel instantaneous and certain. | Separate public method story from evidence grade. |
| Prize race | Frames scholarship as competition and public collaboration. | Research-organization evidence. |
| First word / first scroll | Creates milestone memory. | Use only with source and scope. |
| Resurrected classics | Projects desire for lost authors and works. | Do not identify works without custody. |

## Memory Rule

Public recovery narratives may be important cultural evidence, but they do not
upgrade model outputs, readings, or work identifications.
