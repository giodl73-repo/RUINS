# Refusal Register

- Do not claim exact melody from the tablet.
- Do not claim exact rhythm, tempo, or meter.
- Do not treat modern Western staff notation as the ancient notation itself.
- Do not treat tablature as final recovered performance.
- Do not convert interval names and number signs into certain pitch sequences
  without separate custody.
- Do not treat a modern recording as direct ancient sound.
- Do not use "oldest song" language to imply a fully playable, complete,
  uncontested performance.
- Do not infer complete ritual setting, instrument timbre, or singer practice
  from the current source lock.
