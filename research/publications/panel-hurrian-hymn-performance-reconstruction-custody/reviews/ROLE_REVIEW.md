# Role Review: Hurrian Hymn H6 Performance Reconstruction Custody

**Paper:** panel-hurrian-hymn-performance-reconstruction-custody
**Rubric:** v1.27
**Review date:** 2026-07-06

## Fragment Custodian

The paper separates tablet, text, notation, interval-number signs, damaged
readings, transliteration, staff notation, tablaturization, and recordings.
Exact sign collation and edition claims remain future-locked.

## Absence Historian

The missing object is performance: melody, rhythm, tempo, tuning, timbre, singer
practice, and ritual setting. The paper avoids turning a preserved notation
system into complete recovered sound.

## Reconstruction Skeptic

The refusals are strong and specific. The paper blocks exact melody, exact
rhythm, direct ancient sound, recording equivalence, and "oldest song" overread.

## Cultural Memory Reader

The public appeal of "oldest song" and modern recordings is contained as
afterlife. Tablature is treated as a custody method rather than a magic recovery
of the ancient performance.

## Sibling Boundary Editor

Boundaries are clear: FONTES/MUNDUS gets exact text, CANON gets identities,
PORTO gets geography, RITE gets ritual, CERES gets material/acoustic questions,
musicology systems get final performance theory, and experience systems get
recording/display.

## Paper Editor

The package has a plan, paper, source map, source lock, page-anchor audit, gate,
claim audit, refusal register, memory register, boundary register, and scorecard.

## Safety Editor

No access, looting, or conservation risk. The paper does not instruct handling
or performing from fragile source material as fact.

## Decision

Promote `ruins-capability:notated-performance-reconstruction-custody` from
Candidate to Active. Queue a second unlike case before Core.
