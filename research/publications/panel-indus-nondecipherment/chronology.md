# Chronology: Indus Script Non-Decipherment

**Owner:** STRATA
**Status:** working chronology

This chronology is a layer map, not a final historical chronology.

## Layers

| Layer | What changes | Evidence condition | RUINS caution |
|---|---|---|---|
| Production layer | Indus communities make and use inscribed artifacts. | direct material through surviving artifacts | Artifact use is not automatically text reading. |
| Survival layer | Short inscriptions survive on durable objects. | direct material | Durable survival biases what we can see. |
| Context-loss layer | Spoken language, long texts, and administrative explanations do not survive clearly. | absent / contextual | Absence of long texts weakens translation claims. |
| Discovery/catalog layer | Excavation, publication, sign lists, and corpus catalogs organize the evidence. | copied / reported | Modern sign categories may not match ancient categories. |
| Interpretation layer | Linguistic, non-linguistic, Dravidian, economic, and statistical hypotheses compete. | reconstruction / speculative | Hypotheses must be graded separately from traces. |
| Public-memory layer | The script becomes a famous ancient mystery. | cultural memory | Mystery status is an afterlife, not evidence of meaning. |

## Broad Date/Sequence Lock

| Date/range | Layer | Evidence condition | Source posture | Claim grade |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| c. 2600-1900 BCE | Mature Harappan production/use context for the sign system in many summaries and quantitative papers. | contextual / direct material through artifacts | Use as broad working range, not exact boundary for every artifact. | Corroborated broadly |
| 1977 | Mahadevan M77 concordance becomes a major corpus/sign-list reference. | copied / catalog evidence | Use source-specific M77 values. | Observed as source history |
| 2004 | Farmer/Sproat/Witzel publish major non-linguistic critique. | interpretive critique | Treat as serious critique, not settled result. | Observed as debate layer |
| 2009 | Rao/Yadav et al. publish statistical n-gram analysis. | statistical reconstruction | Supports structure claims, not translation. | Observed as debate layer |
| 2018 | Rao proposes economic/rationing interpretation for some tablets/seals. | interpretive hypothesis | Use as administrative-use hypothesis only. | Observed as debate layer |
| 2026 | Nair preprint tests synthetic non-linguistic baselines. | current preprint / method stress test | Current pressure source, not consensus. | Observed as debate layer |
