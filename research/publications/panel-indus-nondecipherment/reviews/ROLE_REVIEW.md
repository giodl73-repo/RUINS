| Absence Historian | The paper names the missing bridges: accepted reading, secure language bridge, bilingual anchor, long-text context, and ancient explanation of use. It does not turn absence into total ignorance. | Clear |

| Cultural Memory Reader | Public mystery, AI-breakthrough, prize/challenge, identity, and scholarly-debate framings are treated as afterlife evidence, not translation evidence. | Clear |
