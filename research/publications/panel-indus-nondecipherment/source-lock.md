## Paper Use Rules

1. Use exact numeric values only with source labels, for example "in M77 as used
   by Yadav et al."
2. Do not combine sign counts from different catalog traditions into a single
   universal count.
3. Treat statistical structure as structure, not translation.
4. Treat both linguistic and non-linguistic classifications as live
   interpretations unless a source is explicitly being summarized.
5. Treat public summaries as cultural-memory or bibliography aids, not final
   authority.
6. Use [`custody-register.md`](custody-register.md) before promoting a corpus,
   concordance, or public-summary claim to stronger evidence status.
