# Claim Audit

| Claim | Grade | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Jerusalem archaeomagnetism is a RUINS destruction-layer custody case. | Strong | It reconstructs chronology from burned archaeological materials. |
| Archaeomagnetism can support bounded dating and correlation claims. | Strong | This is the method claim under feature, heating, and calibration controls. |
| A magnetic signal is an exact destruction date. | Refused | Magnetic dating requires curves, uncertainty, and independent anchors. |
| Magnetic similarity proves a military campaign. | Refused | Campaign attribution requires historical and regional synthesis. |
| Destruction-layer magnetism confirms a text. | Refused | Textual confirmation is a separate source and interpretation claim. |
| Archaeomagnetism replaces radiocarbon. | Refused | Methods are complementary and separately bounded. |
| Public biblical-proof memory is archaeology. | Refused | Public proof stories are memory surfaces, not evidence conclusions. |
