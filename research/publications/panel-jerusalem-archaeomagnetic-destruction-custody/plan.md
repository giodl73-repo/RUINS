# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use archaeomagnetic destruction layers without turning burned
features into exact dates, campaign proof, or textual confirmation?

## Scope

- Use the 2020 Jerusalem Babylonian-destruction archaeomagnetic paper as anchor.
- Use the 2022 Levant military-campaign paper as regional correlation pressure.
- Use archaeomagnetic dating method summaries as control pressure.
- Separate feature, heating, cooling position, disturbance, orientation,
  mineralogy, measurement, curve, anchor, event claim, synthesis, and memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact destruction date from magnetism alone.
- No certain campaign attribution from magnetic similarity alone.
- No direct confirmation of biblical or historical text.
- No conquest map, complete site chronology, or radiocarbon replacement.
