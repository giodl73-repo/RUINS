# Refusal Register

- Do not treat archaeomagnetism as an exact destruction date.
- Do not infer certain campaign attribution from magnetic similarity alone.
- Do not use a destruction layer as direct textual confirmation.
- Do not make one fired feature a complete site chronology.
- Do not treat archaeomagnetic dating as a replacement for radiocarbon or history.
- Do not turn matched layers into a complete conquest map.
- Do not let biblical-proof headlines become archaeology.
