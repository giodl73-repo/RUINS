## Anchor Limits

- UNESCO can anchor official value and broad site history, not exact household
  microsequence.
- Archaeobotanical evidence can anchor plant-context questions, not a complete
  economy without method-specific source custody.
- "Pompeii of the Americas" anchors reception, not evidence equivalence.
