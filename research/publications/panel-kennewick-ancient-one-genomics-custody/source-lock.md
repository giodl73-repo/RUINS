# Source Lock

Locked claims:

- The genome paper sequenced Kennewick Man to approximately 1x coverage.
- The paper found him closer to modern Native Americans than to any other
  extant population worldwide.
- The paper treats earlier morphology-based non-Native affinity claims as not
  supported by the genome analysis.
- Burke Museum states that the remains were returned in 2017 to tribes who
  claimed him as an ancestor.
- Public scholarship frames the case as an end to a non-Indian ancient North
  America narrative.

Future-locked:

- Tribal authority, law, or repatriation policy conclusions.
- Direct descent from any named living person or community.
- Complete cultural continuity or complete early-Americas population history.
