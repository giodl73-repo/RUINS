## Result

The current source-locked claims have public anchors. The remaining limitations
are scope limits rather than publication blockers:

1. no room-by-room reconstruction claims,
2. no fresco catalog or originality claim without fragment-level custody,
3. no conservation advice,
4. no claim that UNESCO validates Evans's restoration choices,
5. no general Minoan historical synthesis,
6. no claim that digital models recover the Bronze Age palace exactly.
