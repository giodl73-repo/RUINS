# Plan: Knossos Restoration Projection

## Question

Where does the restoration of Knossos become a modern artifact with its own
evidence status, rather than a transparent window onto the Minoan Bronze Age?

## Deliverables

1. Separate Bronze Age fabric, excavated traces, Evans-era reconstruction,
   later conservation, and visitor-facing heritage interpretation.
2. Treat columns, colors, frescoes, concrete supports, and room identities as
   claim surfaces, not neutral scenery.
3. Refuse both extremes: "Knossos is fake" and "the restored palace is simply
   what Minoans built."
4. Track how Minos, labyrinth, bull imagery, and "Minoan civilization" became
   cultural memory.
5. Explain how UNESCO / heritage recognition relates to modern custody, not to
   automatic validation of every restoration choice.

## First Pass Scope

This pass is a working paper. It names major source targets and claim
boundaries, but does not yet source-lock Evans excavation reports, restoration
campaigns, UNESCO inscription language, or conservation scholarship.

## Out Of Scope

- Travel guide or site itinerary.
- Complete Minoan history.
- Linear A / Linear B decipherment.
- Full fresco catalog.
- Conservation advice or restoration instructions.
