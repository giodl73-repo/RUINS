# Source Lock: Knossos Restoration Projection

**Owner:** PALIMPSEST
**Status:** first source lock
**Date:** 2026-07-04

This source lock defines what RUINS may say about Knossos in this paper. It
separates Bronze Age site claims, Evans-era restoration, modern conservation,
heritage recognition, and cultural-memory projection. A source is locked for
bounded use only; it does not validate every visible surface as ancient.

## Paper Use Rules

1. Label visible surfaces as Bronze Age fabric, Evans-era restoration, later
   conservation, replica/completion, heritage interpretation, or cultural
   memory.
2. Do not call the restored palace a transparent Bronze Age original.
3. Do not call the whole site fake because extensive restoration exists.
4. Use UNESCO only for serial heritage and significance claims.
5. Keep specific room/fresco claims source-labeled and deferred until page
   audit.
6. Treat Minos, Labyrinth, Minotaur, and bull imagery as cultural-memory
   surfaces unless independently source-locked.
