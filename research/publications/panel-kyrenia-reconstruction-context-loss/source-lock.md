## Paper Use Rules

1. Treat reconstruction as modern custody, not object-equivalence.
2. Treat cargo origin and date ranges as bounded evidence, not full route or
   voyage narrative.
3. Treat final-report publisher summary as publication-scope custody and
   future exact-claim map, not closure for exact claims.
4. Treat virtual museum/catalog surfaces as modern research afterlife.
5. Do not provide site-location, diving, recovery, collecting, or handling
   guidance.
