# Refusal Register: Laetoli Footprint Trackway Custody

**Owner:** ERRATA
**Status:** publication-ready register

| Refused claim | Why refused | Safer wording |
|---|---|---|
| These footprints are complete individuals. | Footprints are contact traces, not bodies. | Trackmakers or footprint makers. |
| Site G absolutely proves Au. afarensis. | Source wording is likely/most likely and depends on regional fossil context. | Most likely Au. afarensis. |
| Site A settles all hominin locomotion. | Nature frames a study-specific comparative inference. | Evidence for locomotor diversity at Laetoli. |
| Trackways prove family or social intention. | Spatial proximity is not kinship or intent. | Multiple trackways or trackmakers. |
| A scan or cast is equivalent to the trackway. | It is mediated access to a trace. | Digital model, photograph, cast, or scan. |
| Laetoli is the first complete human story. | Public origin memory exceeds the trace evidence. | Early bipedal footprint evidence. |
