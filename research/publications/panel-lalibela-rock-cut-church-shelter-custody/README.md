# Lalibela Rock-Cut Church Shelter Custody

Status: publication-ready
Track: Rock-Cut Landscapes and Erosion
Rubric: v1.22

Lalibela is a RUINS second test for rock-cut erosion custody. Unlike Petra's
sandstone caravan city and facade/water-system problem, Lalibela is a living
rock-hewn church complex where monolithic form, trenches, drainage, water
damage, temporary shelters, visual integrity, pilgrimage, and conservation
management must remain separate.
