# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS score an overbuilt urban ruin when the public sees relocated
and reconstructed remains inside a modern headquarters display?

## Scope

- London Mithraeum discovery, relocation, reconstruction, and current display.
- Historic England listing details for reconstruction status.
- MOLA account of heritage management and reconstruction custody.
- London Mithraeum public display and artefact claims.

## Out Of Scope

- Complete Mithraic ritual interpretation.
- Complete Roman London reconstruction.
- Exact excavation stratigraphy.
- Full artefact catalogue.
- Experience-design evaluation beyond custody status.

## Deliverables

- Publication draft.
- Relocation/reconstruction gate.
- Source lock and page-anchor audit.
- Claim audit and refusal register.
- Role review and scorecard.
- Active-to-Core capability promotion.
