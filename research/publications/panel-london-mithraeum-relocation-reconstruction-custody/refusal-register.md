# Refusal Register

RUINS refuses these current-scope claims:

1. The reconstructed display is the unmediated Roman temple.
2. Relocation or return restores complete original context.
3. Modern mortar, render, wood, light, sound, or haze is Roman evidence.
4. The current route proves exact Mithraic ritual practice.
5. The artefact assemblage gives a complete Roman London inventory.
6. The older listed reconstruction was accurate in position and orientation.
7. Public queues or famous-discovery status prove historical completeness.
8. Listing or display means conservation and interpretation are finished.
