# Source Lock

## Current Lock

Current publication claims are locked to official London Mithraeum, Historic
England, and MOLA pages. The paper may say:

- The current cultural hub returns the temple to the location of its discovery.
- The display includes reconstructed remains, recent excavation artefacts, and
  contemporary/immersive presentation.
- The site was discovered in 1954 during post-Blitz excavation work.
- The earlier listed reconstruction was above and northwest of the original
  site, with different orientation and some modern reconstruction materials.
- MOLA characterizes the older reconstruction as away from the original findspot
  and not entirely sympathetic to the original remains.
- Public memory around the 1954 discovery is a reception layer.
