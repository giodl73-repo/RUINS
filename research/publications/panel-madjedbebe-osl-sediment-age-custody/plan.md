# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use OSL sediment ages without turning sediment burial into exact
arrival, artefact, route, extinction, or peopling-history claims?

## Scope

- Use the 2017 Nature Madjedbebe paper as primary trigger.
- Use OSL method reviews as bleaching, dose, and uncertainty pressure.
- Separate sample, grain population, light exposure, burial context, dose model,
  dose-rate environment, stratigraphy, artefact association, occupation
  inference, regional synthesis, and memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact first-arrival date from OSL alone.
- No exact artefact age from sediment age.
- No migration route, genetics, or megafauna-causation claim.
- No complete Australian/Sahul peopling model.
