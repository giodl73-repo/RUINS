# Refusal Register

- Do not treat OSL age as exact human arrival date.
- Do not treat sediment age as exact artefact age.
- Do not infer certain in-situ association from proximity alone.
- Do not treat stratigraphic integrity as complete site integrity.
- Do not turn first-arrival timing into a migration route.
- Do not use occupation overlap as megafauna-extinction causation.
- Do not let first-arrival headlines become complete peopling history.
