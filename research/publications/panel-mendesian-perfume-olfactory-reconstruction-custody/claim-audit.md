# Claim Audit

| Claim | Grade | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Mendesian is an olfactory-reconstruction custody case. | Strong | Sources anchor production archaeology, formulas, paleobotany, experimental reconstruction, and exhibition scent. |
| The recreated perfume is the exact ancient smell. | Refused | Ingredient identity, quantities, processing, storage, aging, and perception remain future-locked. |
| Tell Timai archaeology helps reconstruct manufacture. | Strong | UH, NTU, and Tell Timai sources all support production context. |
| Amphora residue proves the complete perfume recipe. | Refused | Residue analysis is an evidence layer, not a full recipe or smell. |
| Cleopatra personally wore this reconstructed batch. | Refused | Current sources support public framing and possibility language, not personal-use proof. |
| Exhibition scent is direct access to ancient atmosphere. | Refused | Museum scent is an access layer and modern sensory interpretation. |
