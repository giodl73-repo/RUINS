# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS grade a recreated ancient smell when the evidence comes from
production archaeology, residue analysis, textual formulas, paleobotany,
experimental batches, and museum display?

## Evidence Surfaces

- Thmuis / Tell Timai perfume-industry archaeology.
- Kilns, perfume bottles, glass unguentaria, and liquid-production area.
- Amphora residue analysis.
- Ancient Greek formulas and classical witnesses.
- Paleobotanical ingredient identifications.
- Experimental archaeology and modern batches.
- Ingredient substitution, process time, and storage conditions.
- Exhibition scent and audience experience.
- Cleopatra and "smell history" public memory.

## Exclusions

- Full Ptolemaic or Roman Egypt history.
- Exact ancient recipe.
- Modern perfumery instructions.
- Chemical protocol or safety guidance.
- Cleopatra biography.
- Exhibition design review.

## Promotion Target

Promote `ruins-capability:olfactory-reconstruction-custody` from Candidate to
Active with a concrete gate and refusal register.
