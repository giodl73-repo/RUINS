# Claim Audit

| Claim | Grade | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Tree rings can provide annual dates for sampled timbers when crossdated. | Strong | Method claim stays at sample/date level. |
| Mesa Verde tree-ring samples can support bounded construction and repair histories. | Strong | Requires room, feature, and terminal-ring context. |
| Dated timbers alone prove exact building completion. | Refused | Construction use is separate from felling and sample date. |
| Last dated timber proves abandonment. | Refused | Abandonment requires independent occupation and deposition custody. |
| Drought rings explain migration or collapse. | Refused | Climate signal is not social causation. |
| Timber provenance proves a complete trade system. | Refused | Origin signal is not a network map. |
