# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use annual tree-ring precision without turning dated wood into a
complete building, occupation, drought, or settlement story?

## Scope

- Use dendrochronology method reviews as annual-resolution pressure.
- Use Mesa Verde/Southwest examples as construction and occupation pressure.
- Use timber-provenance and old-wood discussions as reuse and trade pressure.
- Separate sample, tree, ring sequence, terminal ring, felling, reuse,
  construction, chronology, provenance, synthesis, and memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact building completion unless construction context is separately locked.
- No complete occupation span or abandonment date from last dated wood.
- No drought-causation, migration, community-size, or whole-settlement history.
- No timber-trade map or forest-management system from provenance alone.
