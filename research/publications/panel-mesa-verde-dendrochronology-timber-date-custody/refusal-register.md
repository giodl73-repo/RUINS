# Refusal Register

- Do not treat terminal ring as exact building completion.
- Do not treat a felling cluster as a complete construction sequence.
- Do not treat dated wood as a full occupation span.
- Do not treat last dated timber as abandonment.
- Do not treat drought rings as migration or collapse proof.
- Do not treat beam counts as community size.
- Do not treat timber provenance as a complete trade map.
- Do not treat reused wood as direct room-use chronology.
