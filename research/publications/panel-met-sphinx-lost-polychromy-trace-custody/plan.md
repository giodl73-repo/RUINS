# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS score a surviving stone object when its lost color layer is
known through pigment traces, imaging, chemical analysis, reconstruction, and
public color memory?

## Scope

- Met Chroma exhibition overview.
- Archaic Greek sphinx finial research.
- Pigment traces and scientific methods.
- Physical reconstruction and AR experience.
- Modern white-marble reception pressure.

## Out Of Scope

- Complete color scheme for every ancient sculpture.
- Full Greek aesthetic history.
- Exact conservation science beyond source-scoped methods.
- Final funerary or ritual meaning.
- Object-equivalent restoration claims.

## Deliverables

- Publication draft.
- Lost-polychromy trace gate.
- Source lock and page-anchor audit.
- Claim audit and refusal register.
- Role review and scorecard.
- Candidate-to-active capability promotion.
