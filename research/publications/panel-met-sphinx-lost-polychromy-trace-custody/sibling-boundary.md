# Sibling Boundary

| Surface | Owner | RUINS boundary |
|---|---|---|
| Sphinx, stele, exhibition objects, reconstructions | CANON | RUINS does not become the object identity registry. |
| Pigments, marble, samples, imaging, XRF, microscopy, conservation | CERES | RUINS records trace custody, not conservation science. |
| Funerary meaning and symbolic protector role | RITE | RUINS refuses final ritual interpretation. |
| Greek art history and later reception | MAXIM / LUCIA | RUINS keeps history as context and memory. |
| Exhibition, replica, and gallery interpretation | Experience systems | RUINS records display status, not exhibit design. |
| 3D scan and AR implementation | Digital systems | RUINS treats digital experience as mediated access. |
| Exact scholarly catalogue and object records | FONTES / CANON | RUINS source-locks only current publication claims. |
