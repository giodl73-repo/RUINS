# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS grade a modern drink reconstructed from ancient vessel residues
when public tasting and commercial production make it feel like direct ancient
taste?

## Evidence Surfaces

- Drinking-vessel residue.
- Funerary banquet context.
- Grape wine, barley beer, and honey mead ingredient markers.
- Tomb attribution and King Midas/Gordias memory.
- Recipe inference from residue.
- Modern brewing choices.
- Commercial product identity.
- Tasting event and public experience.

## Exclusions

- Alcohol recipe or brewing instructions.
- Exact chemical marker interpretation.
- Full Phrygian history.
- Final tomb identification.
- Product review.
- Drinking recommendation or safety guidance.

## Promotion Target

Promote `ruins-capability:gustatory-reconstruction-custody` from Candidate to
Active with a concrete gate and refusal register.
