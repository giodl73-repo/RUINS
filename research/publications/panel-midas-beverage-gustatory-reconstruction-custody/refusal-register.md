# Refusal Register

- Do not call Midas Touch the exact ancient drink.
- Do not turn residue markers into complete recipe, proportions, or process.
- Do not infer yeast, water, fermentation temperature, or serving conditions.
- Do not claim Midas or Gordias personally consumed the reconstructed beverage.
- Do not treat a commercial bottle as direct access to a funerary banquet.
- Do not provide brewing instructions, alcohol guidance, or safety claims.
- Do not use King Midas legend as archaeological evidence.
