# Dental Calculus Gate

| Layer | Milk-protein custody question |
|---|---|
| Specimen | Which individual, tooth, region, date, and calculus sample produced the peptide? |
| Matrix | Is the signal mineralized plaque protein, DNA, microremain, isotope, or mixed evidence? |
| Method | Which mass spectrometry and protein-identification workflow produced the hit? |
| Protein | Is beta-lactoglobulin confidently assigned and distinguishable from contamination? |
| Species | Is cattle, sheep, goat, or other milk source assigned, and at what confidence? |
| Consumption | Is the claim exposure, consumption, repeated consumption, or population pattern? |
| Adaptation | Are lactase persistence, herding, economy, and culture separately sourced? |
| Memory | Is milk-origin public history separated from peptide evidence? |
