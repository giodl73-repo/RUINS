# Refusal Register

- Do not treat beta-lactoglobulin as an exact meal ledger.
- Do not treat milk-consumption evidence as complete dairying economy.
- Do not infer lactase persistence from milk protein alone.
- Do not collapse species-source peptide assignment and herd management.
- Do not turn individual evidence into whole-population practice without scale controls.
- Do not let milk-origin public history outrun peptide evidence.
