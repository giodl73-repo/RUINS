# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS score a destroyed monument when the void is filled by a
source-controlled reconstruction that also becomes a peace and recovery symbol?

## Scope

- UNESCO World Heritage description and Outstanding Universal Value.
- 1990s destruction and 2004 reconstruction.
- Authenticity, documentation, materials, exposed remnants, and museum display.
- Reconciliation and international-cooperation memory.

## Out Of Scope

- Full Bosnian war history.
- Engineering design details.
- Complete bridge-stone inventory.
- Legal responsibility for destruction.
- Community consensus beyond source-scoped statements.

## Deliverables

- Publication draft.
- Reconstructed-void gate.
- Source lock and page-anchor audit.
- Claim audit and refusal register.
- Role review and scorecard.
- Active-to-Core capability promotion.
