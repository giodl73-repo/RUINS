# Refusal Register

RUINS refuses these current-scope claims:

1. The rebuilt Old Bridge is the complete original bridge.
2. Authentic materials and techniques make reconstruction object-equivalent.
3. Displayed remnants prove a complete original-fabric inventory.
4. Rebuilding erases the missing-span destruction history.
5. Reconciliation symbolism proves community consensus.
6. International cooperation settles final political or legal meaning.
7. Restored surroundings mean the whole historic city is fully recovered.
8. World Heritage inscription means conservation is finished.
