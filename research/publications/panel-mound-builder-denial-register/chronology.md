## Chronology Rules

1. Do not collapse all mound-building periods into Hopewell.
2. Do not treat nineteenth-century speculation as a live alternative.
3. Do not treat modern heritage recognition as the origin event.
4. Keep destruction, restoration, excavation, and public access as later custody
   layers.
