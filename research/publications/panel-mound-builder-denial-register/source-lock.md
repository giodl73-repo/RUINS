# Source Lock: Mound Builder Denial Register

**Owner:** PALIMPSEST
**Status:** page-audited source lock
**Date:** 2026-07-05

This source lock permits broad, source-scoped claims about the Mound Builder
myth as colonial/racialized denial and about current public-source attribution
of Hopewell and related earthworks to Indigenous American peoples. It does not
authorize detailed tribal continuity, complete site chronologies, excavation
claims, sacred interpretation, or quotations from nineteenth-century sources
without further custody.

Current paper claims are anchored in
[`page-anchor-audit.md`](page-anchor-audit.md).

## Paper Use Rules

1. Treat "Mound Builder" as a historic category and denial narrative, not a
   separate lost people.
2. Attribute high-level Hopewell earthwork construction to American Indian /
   Indigenous communities using source labels.
3. Do not collapse all mounds, sites, periods, or communities into Hopewell.
4. Treat impressive scale, geometry, alignments, and exotic materials as
   achievement/contact evidence, not outsider-authorship evidence.
5. Treat sacred and community memory with care and hand exact identity or
   protocol claims to sibling systems.
6. Do not provide excavation, collecting, looting, or site-disturbance guidance.
