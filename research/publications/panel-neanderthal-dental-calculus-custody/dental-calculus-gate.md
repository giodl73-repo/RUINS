# Dental Calculus Gate

| Layer | Custody question |
|---|---|
| Specimen | Which individual, tooth, site, and calculus deposit was sampled? |
| Matrix | What kind of plaque, mineralized residue, microremain, or biomolecule is present? |
| Sampling | How were sampling, extraction, controls, and contamination handled? |
| Method | Is the evidence DNA, protein, microfossil, isotope, chemistry, or mixed assay? |
| Taxon | How confident is the plant, animal, microbial, or pathogen assignment? |
| Diet | Is the claim dietary possibility, repeated food, exact meal, or regional pattern? |
| Health | Is the claim microbiome profile, pathogen signal, disease, or treatment? |
| Behaviour | Does medicine, ecology, intimacy, or lifestyle remain separately sourced? |
| Memory | Are public ancient-life headlines kept separate from trace evidence? |
