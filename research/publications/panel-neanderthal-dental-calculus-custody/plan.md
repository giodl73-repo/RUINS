# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS score diet, health, and behaviour reconstructions from ancient
dental calculus without turning taxonomic hits into life stories?

## Scope

- Use Weyrich et al. 2017 as primary source.
- Use later dental-calculus caution work as pressure against overclaiming.
- Separate calculus matrix, sequencing, taxonomic assignment, contamination,
  microbiome, diet, pathogen, behaviour, medicine, and public memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No clinical diagnosis.
- No exact meal or deliberate medicine claim.
- No species-wide Neanderthal lifestyle synthesis.
