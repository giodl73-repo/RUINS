# Refusal Register

- Do not treat calculus DNA as an exact meal ledger.
- Do not treat plant signal as deliberate medicine without separate evidence.
- Do not turn pathogen or microbiome signal into full diagnosis.
- Do not ignore taxonomic assignment, authentication, or contamination limits.
- Do not generalize from a few individuals to all Neanderthals.
- Do not let public ancient-life headlines set the claim grade.
