# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS score a famous astronomical alignment without turning the
light event into total function or design certainty?

## Scope

- Use National Museum, World Heritage Ireland, and Newgrange report summaries.
- Separate fabric, reconstruction, observation, simulation, interpretation, and
  memory.
- Activate a reusable astronomical-alignment reconstruction capability.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact solstice-day prediction model.
- No final ritual/funerary interpretation.
- No tourism, access, or conservation guidance.
