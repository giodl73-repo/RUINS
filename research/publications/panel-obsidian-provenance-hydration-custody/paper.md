# Obsidian Source Matches and Hydration Custody

Obsidian is a strong RUINS trigger because volcanic glass can often be matched
to geological sources through geochemical fingerprints. XRF, pXRF, INAA, and
related methods can support bounded claims about source assignment and movement
of raw material or artefacts. Hydration rims can add chronological pressure when
rate models, water content, and temperature history are controlled.

The evidence is powerful but bounded. A source match is not a trade route. A
hydration rim is not an exact artefact age. Source distance is not a migration
path. Flake counts and cortex patterns do not by themselves prove market scale,
workshop organization, procurement system, ethnic identity, territorial
boundary, political alliance, or complete interaction sphere.

RUINS promotes obsidian provenance-hydration custody to Core here. Obsidian
source and hydration signals can support bounded provenance, dating-pressure,
and reduction claims, but exchange, mobility, chronology, identity, and network
synthesis require separate custody.
