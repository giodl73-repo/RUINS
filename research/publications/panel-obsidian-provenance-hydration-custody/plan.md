# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use obsidian source matching and hydration dating without turning
geochemical clusters into full exchange networks or exact artefact histories?

## Scope

- Use XRF/pXRF and laboratory sourcing papers as provenance pressure.
- Use hydration-dating reviews and Peru case studies as chronology pressure.
- Use sub-source and source-distance work as exchange-network pressure.
- Separate artefact, source, method, calibration, assignment, hydration,
  context, reduction, exchange, synthesis, and memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No direct trade route from source assignment alone.
- No exact artefact age from hydration rim alone.
- No migration path, ethnic identity, territorial boundary, market network,
  political alliance, or complete interaction sphere.
