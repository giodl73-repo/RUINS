# Source Lock

Locked claims:

- Obsidian artefacts can often be matched to geological sources through
  geochemical characterization when source libraries and calibration support the
  assignment.
- Portable XRF can be useful for non-destructive source analysis, but instrument
  differences, calibration, and ambiguous specimens require custody.
- Obsidian hydration dating depends on rim measurement, diffusion modelling,
  intrinsic water, source chemistry, and thermal history.
- Source assignment and hydration pressure can support bounded provenance and
  chronology claims, but exchange, identity, route, and network claims require
  separate synthesis.

Future-locked:

- Direct trade route.
- Exact artefact age.
- Migration path.
- Ethnic identity or territorial boundary.
- Complete procurement, market, workshop, or alliance system.
