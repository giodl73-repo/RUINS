# Claim Audit

| Claim | Grade | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Experimental lighting is a RUINS reconstruction-custody case. | Strong | It reconstructs lost visual conditions through modern experiments and model transfer. |
| Torches, lamps, and fireplaces have different affordances. | Strong | Source explicitly compares lighting systems. |
| The experiment recovers ancient perception. | Refused | Modern setup, fuel choices, observer behavior, and cave conditions mediate the result. |
| Visibility can inform cave-art placement hypotheses. | Moderate | Useful as a constrained hypothesis, not intention proof. |
| Smoke and movement matter to interpretation. | Strong | They are part of the measured/reported behavior. |
| The findings settle ritual meaning. | Refused | Meaning belongs to separately sourced interpretation. |
