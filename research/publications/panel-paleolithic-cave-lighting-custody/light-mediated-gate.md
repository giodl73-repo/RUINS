# Light-Mediated Reconstruction Gate

| Layer | Custody question |
|---|---|
| Trace | What archaeological evidence anchors a lighting system? |
| Fuel | Which fuel, lamp, torch, hearth, wick, fat, resin, or wood is selected? |
| Experiment | What modern setup, cave context, measurement, and observer position are used? |
| Behavior | What is measured for intensity, duration, smoke, heat, relighting, and movement? |
| Transfer | Is the result applied directly, through 3D/GIS, VR, or another model? |
| Visibility | What surface, distance, angle, route, or activity becomes visible? |
| Interpretation | Does visibility stay separate from art meaning, ritual, route, and intention? |
| Memory | Is public "seeing the past" language labelled as access shorthand? |
