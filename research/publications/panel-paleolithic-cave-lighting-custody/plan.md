# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS score research that reconstructs ancient visibility from
experimental firelight?

## Scope

- Use the PLOS ONE experimental cave-lighting paper as the source-locked anchor.
- Separate archaeological traces, fuel selection, combustion behavior,
  measurement, model transfer, visibility, movement, and meaning.
- Activate a reusable light-mediated reconstruction capability.

## Out Of Scope

- No instructions for lighting fires in caves.
- No route, ritual, or image-meaning synthesis.
- No claim that experimental lighting recovers ancient perception.
