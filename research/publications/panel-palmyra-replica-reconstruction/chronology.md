# Chronology: Palmyra Replica Reconstruction

**Owner:** CALENDAR
**Status:** working chronology

| Layer | Event/process | RUINS significance |
|---|---|---|
| 1980 | Palmyra is inscribed as a World Heritage property. | Site value is governed by UNESCO OUV attributes. |
| 2013-present | Palmyra is on the World Heritage in Danger list. | Damage and risk precede a single reconstruction episode. |
| 2015 | The Arch of Triumph and other monuments are destroyed or damaged. | Destruction claims require feature-specific custody. |
| 2016 | A scale replica/facsimile of the Triumphal Arch is installed in London. | Public replica becomes a new memory object. |
| 2019-2020 | UNESCO recovery meeting urges first-aid posture and detailed assessment before reconstruction. | Governance prevents replica-as-recovery drift. |
| 2024 | World Heritage Committee decision records restoration/reconstruction methodology needs. | Restoration is process-bound, not automatic. |

## Chronology Rule

Do not collapse destruction, documentation, replica production, public display,
emergency conservation, and authorized reconstruction into one recovery event.
