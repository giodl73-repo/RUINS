# Refusal Register: Palmyra Replica Reconstruction

**Owner:** ERRATA / Safety Editor
**Status:** working refusal register

| Refused claim | Reason |
|---|---|
| A replica is the original. | Original fabric, site context, and replica object status differ. |
| A public display is site restoration. | Restoration is governance- and field-assessment-bound. |
| A digital model is conservation approval. | Models are mediated documentation, not approval. |
| Palmyra has been rebuilt. | Recovery is feature-specific and process-bound. |
| One monument stands for the whole site. | The site has multiple attributes and damage layers. |
| RUINS can advise site conservation. | Conservation advice belongs to qualified heritage authorities. |
