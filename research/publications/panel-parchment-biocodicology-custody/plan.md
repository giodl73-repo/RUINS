# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use parchment biomolecular evidence without turning folio-level
species or DNA signals into provenance, scriptorium, trade, library, or textual
authority claims?

## Scope

- Use the Heritage Science biocodicology field guide as the primary trigger.
- Use ZooMS and medieval-library biocodicology papers as method pressure.
- Separate object, folio, sampling, conservation, material, molecular
  assignment, species call, production inference, textual boundary, and memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No complete manuscript provenance from one species call.
- No scriptorium attribution or trade route without sibling-owned evidence.
- No herd history from collagen peptides.
- No text, edition, authorship, date, or authority claim from material evidence.
