# Refusal Register

- Do not treat a species call as exact animal identity.
- Do not treat a folio sample as complete manuscript provenance.
- Do not treat ZooMS as scriptorium attribution.
- Do not infer trade routes from parchment DNA alone.
- Do not turn collagen peptides into herd history.
- Do not treat microbiome signal as complete use history.
- Do not let material evidence become textual authority.
