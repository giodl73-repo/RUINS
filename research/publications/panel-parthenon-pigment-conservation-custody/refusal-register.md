# Refusal Register

RUINS refuses these current-scope claims:

1. Visible pigment gives the complete original color scheme.
2. VIL or XRF output is direct access to final ancient appearance.
3. Egyptian blue traces supply the full original palette.
4. Cleaning and conservation restore ancient color.
5. Display access creates whole-object certainty.
6. Pigment evidence settles divine, ritual, or iconographic meaning.
7. Parthenon traces define all Greek sculpture aesthetics.
8. The paper can settle Parthenon legal/provenance disputes.
