## Paper Use Rules

1. Distinguish object group, fragment category, and institution.
2. Treat removal/acquisition claims as source-scoped provenance surfaces.
3. Separate legal title, loan, return request, discussion, display, copy, and
   physical movement.
4. Treat plaster copies as replicas/absence markers, not object equivalents.
5. Do not decide legal title or final diplomatic settlement.
