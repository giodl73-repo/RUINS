# Plan: Pompeii Cast Void Custody

**Owner:** ASHLAR
**Status:** publication-ready
**Date:** 2026-07-06

## Problem

The Pompeii body casts look like direct bodies, but they are technical objects:
voids left after decomposition were filled, displayed, restored, scanned,
sampled, and narrated. Some casts contain skeletal material, and recent DNA
work challenges family and gender stories built from posture, jewelry, and
display.

## RUINS Claim

Pompeii casts require cast-void identity custody. A cavity, plaster cast,
skeletal sample, DNA result, display placement, and public tragedy image are
different evidence layers. None alone proves identity, kinship, emotion,
cause-of-death detail, or the social profile of Pompeii.

## Work Surface

1. Lock official Pompeii claims for eruption process, void formation, number of
   casts, display, damage, restoration, scanning, and 3D copies.
2. Lock Current Biology / Max Planck claims for DNA from skeletal material in
   casts and challenged identity/kinship assumptions.
3. Build a cast-void custody gate.
4. Refuse body-equivalence, frozen-final-moment certainty, family tableau,
   gender-from-jewelry, emotion-from-posture, and population generalization.
5. Promote `ruins-capability:cast-void-identity-custody` from Candidate to
   Active.
