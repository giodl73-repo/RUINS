# Refusal Register: Pompeii Cast Void Custody

**Owner:** ERRATA
**Status:** publication-ready register

| Refused claim | Why refused | Safer wording |
|---|---|---|
| The casts are the victims' bodies. | They are plaster/resin casts of voids, though some contain skeletal material. | Casted absences or body casts. |
| Pompeii is frozen exactly at the instant of death. | The evidence passed through eruption, decay, casting, restoration, display, and study. | Preserved and mediated traces of the disaster. |
| Physical closeness proves family. | DNA results challenge some kinship stories based on proximity. | Display or find group unless kinship is independently locked. |
| Jewelry proves a woman. | The DNA study warns against gendered assumptions from appearance or artifacts. | Jewelry-bearing individual unless sex is source-locked. |
| Posture proves agony, prayer, protection, or love. | Mental state is not directly recoverable from pose. | Posture or position at preservation/casting. |
| The sampled casts reveal all Pompeiians. | Sample size and selection are limited. | Selected sampled individuals in the study. |
