# Source Lock: Qin Book Burning Textual Destruction

**Owner:** PALIMPSEST
**Status:** page-audited source lock
**Date:** 2026-07-05

This source lock permits broad, source-scoped claims about the Qin book-burning
tradition as deliberate textual destruction. It does not authorize exact
Chinese quotation, English translation, destroyed-title inventory, full Qin/Han
history, or total claims about Chinese textual transmission.

Current paper claims are anchored in
[`page-anchor-audit.md`](page-anchor-audit.md).

## Paper Use Rules

1. Say "source tradition" or "Li Si as recorded by Sima Qian" when using the
   policy surface.
2. Treat Columbia wording as translation-mediated and do not quote it as a
   critical edition.
3. Use category language, not destroyed-title or destroyed-count language.
4. Separate book burning from scholar execution.
5. Treat later tyranny/censorship memory as afterlife evidence.
6. Hand exact Chinese text and broader Qin/Han history to sibling systems.
