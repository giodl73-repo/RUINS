# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use radiocarbon dates and Bayesian models without turning sample ages
or model outputs into exact archaeological events?

## Scope

- Use marine and freshwater reservoir-effect sources as offset pressure.
- Use old-wood guidance as sample-life pressure.
- Use Bayesian modelling and outlier papers as model-output pressure.
- Separate sample, carbon source, context, pretreatment, calibration, reservoir,
  old wood, diet, model, synthesis, and memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact event date from calibrated range alone.
- No direct occupation or burial date without association custody.
- No universal reservoir correction.
- No population, migration, collapse, or transition claim from model output alone.
