# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use lake-core pollen and charcoal records without turning proxies into
ecocide, last-tree, or collapse narratives?

## Scope

- Use Rapa Nui deforestation and lake-core debates as the trigger.
- Use pollen, charcoal, diatom, sediment, and isotope proxies as method pressure.
- Separate core, catchment, proxy, preservation, chronology, taxon assignment,
  fire signal, human/climate attribution, scale, social inference, and memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact deforestation date from a pollen curve.
- No whole-island vegetation map from one core.
- No direct human overuse, population collapse, warfare, or famine proof.
- No climate determinism, last-tree story, or moral parable.
