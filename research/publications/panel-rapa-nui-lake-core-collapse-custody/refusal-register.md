# Refusal Register

- Do not treat pollen decline as an exact deforestation date.
- Do not treat one core as a whole-island vegetation map.
- Do not infer direct human overuse from charcoal alone.
- Do not turn vegetation change into population collapse.
- Do not use proxy shifts as warfare, famine, or social breakdown proof.
- Do not treat drought or climate stress as cultural determinism.
- Do not let ecocide or last-tree headlines become complete Rapa Nui history.
