# Claim Audit

| Claim | Grade | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Surface dating can constrain exposure histories. | Strong | Requires method and surface custody. |
| Lichenometry and varnish can support bounded relative or calibrated age discussion. | Bounded | Calibration and disturbance remain explicit. |
| Cosmogenic ages can constrain exposure but not automatically human event dates. | Bounded | Association and inheritance are separate. |
| Exposure age is construction date. | Refused | Exposure event and construction event differ. |
| Rock varnish layer is petroglyph date. | Refused | Varnish formation and carving association are separate. |
| Calibration curve is universal. | Refused | Species, substrate, and region are separate. |
