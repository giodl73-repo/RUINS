# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS score papers that use surface-exposure methods to date stone
monuments, carvings, walls, quarries, paths, or associated landforms?

## Scope

- Cosmogenic nuclides, 10Be, 26Al, 36Cl, exposure ages, burial ages, shielding,
  inheritance, erosion, and calibration.
- Lichenometry, lichen thallus size, growth curves, species/substrate limits,
  and disturbance.
- Rock varnish, varnish microlamination, microerosion, weathering rind, and
  petroglyph/stone-surface chronology pressure.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact monument construction date.
- No direct petroglyph or carving date.
- No final quarry, path-use, abandonment, or cultural phase chronology.
- No universal calibration curve.
- No geomorphology method benchmark.

## Target Artifact

Promote rock-surface exposure dating custody directly to Core with a checklist,
gate, refusal register, sibling-boundary register, and rubric v1.64 cap.
