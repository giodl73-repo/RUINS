|---|---|---|
| Sampled surfaces, monuments, petroglyphs, walls, cairns, quarries, datasets, and publications | CANON | RUINS gates evidence use but does not own entity identity. |
| Cosmogenic chemistry, lichen ecology, rock varnish, microerosion, calibration, shielding, inheritance, and erosion | CERES | RUINS records custody controls, not method implementation. |
| Landform and exposure geography | PORTO | RUINS does not own geomorphic geography. |

| Petroglyph or ritual meaning | RITE | RUINS only audits chronological custody. |
| Oldest-surface and solved-chronology narratives | Public-history systems | RUINS contains public memory. |
