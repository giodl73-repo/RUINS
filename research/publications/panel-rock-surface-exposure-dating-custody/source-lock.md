# Source Lock

## Locked Claims

- Cosmogenic nuclide methods can date surface exposure and require shielding,
  inheritance, erosion, and production-rate controls.
- Lichenometry can be applied to archaeological stone surfaces but depends on
  calibration, species/substrate, sample size, and disturbance history.
- Rock-varnish and microerosion approaches have been used for rock-surface and
  petroglyph chronology but have strong calibration and variability limits.
