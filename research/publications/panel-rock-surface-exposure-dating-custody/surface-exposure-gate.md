# Surface Exposure Gate

| Gate | Required question |
|---|---|
| Sample custody | Which surface, stone, carving, varnish patch, lichen population, or rock layer is measured? |
| Target event | Is the target exposure, quarrying, carving, construction, abandonment, movement, or erosion? |
| Method custody | Is the evidence cosmogenic nuclide, lichenometry, rock varnish, microerosion, or weathering rind? |
| Inheritance custody | Could inherited nuclides, prior exposure, reused stones, older lichen, or pre-existing varnish affect the result? |
| Shielding custody | Are sediment, architecture, vegetation, snow, topography, and burial corrections explicit? |
| Erosion custody | Are spalling, cleaning, conservation, weathering, vandalism, and surface loss separated? |
| Calibration custody | Which growth curve, production model, varnish sequence, or known-age surface supports the estimate? |
| Association custody | Is the dated surface directly tied to the human event or only nearby/compatible? |
| Memory custody | Are oldest-surface, solved-petroglyph, and exact-construction stories contained? |
