# Custody Register: Roman Concrete Partial Survival

**Owner:** PALIMPSEST
**Status:** first custody register
**Date:** 2026-07-04

## Evidence Surfaces

| Surface | Custody state | Paper use | Boundary |
|---|---|---|---|
| Vitruvius / ancient textual tradition | Translation-mediated public text; manuscript transmission is outside current custody. | Establish ancient recipe/material vocabulary and a partial textual witness. | Not direct proof of all field practice. |
| Surviving structures | Material survival mediated by site preservation and sampling access. | Establish that Roman concrete systems endured in specific contexts. | Survival does not prove universal superiority. |
| Marine concrete samples | Laboratory custody in specialist mineralogy papers. | Discuss seawater-driven mineral evolution in marine concrete. | Marine environment only. |
| Lime clasts / hot-mixing samples | Laboratory characterization in materials-science work. | Discuss hot-mixing and self-healing as source-specific models. | Do not turn into a construction recipe. |
| Pompeii construction-site materials | Archaeological context from Regio IX / Domus IX 10, 1 and materials analysis. | Discuss in-situ raw-material piles, unfinished walls, tools, and workflow evidence. | Pompeii-specific; do not universalize empire-wide. |
| Modern Roman-inspired materials | Experimental and commercial/engineering translation. | Track modern afterlife and sustainability claims. | Modern artifact, not ancient concrete. |
| Public lost-secret media | Public interpretation and science communication. | Track cultural memory and myth compression. | Not authority for material claims. |

## Custody Risks

| Risk | Control |
|---|---|
| Treating an ancient text as a complete technical recipe | Label as textual witness and require source context. |
| Treating a mineral mechanism as all Roman concrete | Label marine, wall, Pompeii, sample, or experimental analog. |
| Turning hot-mixing into construction advice | Keep all discussion informational and non-instructional. |
| Converting public summaries into primary evidence | Use primary papers for material claims and summaries for public-memory framing. |
| Flattening modern sustainability claims into ancient performance claims | Route modern claims through cultural memory / engineering translation. |

## Custody Decision

This pass is cleared for a stable working paper about partial technical
survival. It is not cleared for recipe publication, restoration guidance,
structural recommendations, or universal claims about Roman concrete.
