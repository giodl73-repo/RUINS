## Result

The current source-locked claims have public anchors. The remaining limitations
are scope limits rather than publication blockers:

1. no construction, restoration, or structural instructions,
2. no universal Roman concrete recipe,
3. no empire-wide claim from Pompeii or any single sample set,
4. no marine-concrete claim applied to all Roman concrete,
5. no detailed mineral pathway, image, or figure claim without deeper source
   custody,
6. no claim that modern concrete can simply copy ancient practice.
