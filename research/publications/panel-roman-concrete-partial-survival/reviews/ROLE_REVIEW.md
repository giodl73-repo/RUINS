The paper is publication-ready as a RUINS case study of partial technical
survival, modern laboratory reconstruction, and lost-secret refusal. The
remaining future-use findings do not block the current paper because the paper
refuses recipe, construction, universalization, and detailed mineral-pathway
claims.
