# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use cryptotephra marker horizons without turning microscopic volcanic
ash into solved dates, destruction layers, or collapse narratives?

## Scope

- Use Santorini/Thera eruption chronology as the high-pressure trigger.
- Use cryptotephra archaeology reviews as method pressure.
- Use ice-core/sulfate correlation debate as source-attribution pressure.
- Separate shard, context, chemistry, source, stratigraphy, chronology,
  archive correlation, event claim, sibling synthesis, and memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact eruption date from cryptotephra alone.
- No certain source attribution from sulfate or ash without geochemical custody.
- No direct destruction, migration, climate, or collapse claim without siblings.
- No solved Bronze Age chronology.
