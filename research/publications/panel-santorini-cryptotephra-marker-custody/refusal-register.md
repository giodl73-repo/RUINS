# Refusal Register

- Do not treat cryptotephra as an exact eruption date.
- Do not treat sulfate spikes as certain source attribution.
- Do not infer direct destruction from a marker horizon alone.
- Do not turn archive correlation into complete cultural synchronization.
- Do not use tephra alone as migration, climate, or collapse causation.
- Do not make Santorini/Thera chronology solved when date systems remain gated.
- Do not let eruption-year headlines become complete Bronze Age chronology.
