# Refusal Register

- Do not treat complete-looking notation as direct ancient sound.
- Do not infer exact tempo, rhythm, voice, pronunciation, instrument, or
  accompaniment from the current source lock.
- Do not treat modern staff notation as object-equivalent to the ancient signs.
- Do not treat a modern recording as the original performance.
- Do not use the damaged lower line to settle Seikilos, Euterpe, or funerary
  identity claims.
- Do not make one stone inscription stand for all ancient Greek music practice.
- Do not use "oldest complete song" language as proof of complete performance
  context.
