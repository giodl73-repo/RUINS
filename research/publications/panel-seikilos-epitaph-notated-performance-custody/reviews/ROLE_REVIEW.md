# Role Review: Seikilos Epitaph Notated Performance Custody

**Paper:** panel-seikilos-epitaph-notated-performance-custody
**Rubric:** v1.28
**Review date:** 2026-07-06

## Fragment Custodian

The paper separates stone, dedication, poem, note signs, damaged lower line,
comparative corpus, notation-system transmission, modern transcription, and
recordings. Exact Greek edition and technical musicology remain future-locked.

## Absence Historian

The missing performance is clearly named: voice, tempo, rhythm, instrument,
delivery, social setting, and continuous notation tradition. Completeness of the
inscription is not allowed to become completeness of performance.

## Reconstruction Skeptic

The refusals block direct sound, staff-score equivalence, recording equivalence,
complete Greek music practice, and identity overclaims from the damaged lower
line.

## Cultural Memory Reader

"Oldest complete song" is handled as public shorthand. Modern recordings and
classroom scores are access layers, not recoveries of an ancient event.

## Sibling Boundary Editor

Boundaries are clear: FONTES/MUNDUS gets text, CANON gets identities, PORTO gets
place, RITE gets funerary meaning, CERES gets material/instruments, musicology
systems get performance theory, and experience systems get recordings.

## Paper Editor

The package has a plan, paper, source map, source lock, page-anchor audit, gate,
claim audit, refusal register, memory register, boundary register, and scorecard.

## Safety Editor

No access, looting, or conservation risk. The paper stays informational and does
not give handling or conservation advice.

## Decision

Promote `ruins-capability:notated-performance-reconstruction-custody` to Core.
Add `docs/notated-performance-reconstruction-checklist.md` and rubric v1.28 cap.
