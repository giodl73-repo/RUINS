**Track:** Evidence and Absence

This package treats the reconstructed scent of Senetnay's mummification balm as
a RUINS case about residue-based olfactory reconstruction. It separates canopic
jar residue, biomolecular characterization, ingredient identification,
trade-route inference, experimental scent, museum display, and afterlife memory.
