# Paradata Transparency Gate

| Layer | Custody question |
|---|---|
| Source evidence | What physical, textual, measured, archival, or comparative sources feed the reconstruction? |
| Metadata | Are object, file, model, source, version, and author records available? |
| Paradata | Are interpretive decisions, assumptions, alternatives, and reasoning documented? |
| Uncertainty | Are uncertain, hypothetical, restored, and direct layers visually or textually distinguished? |
| Output | What exactly does the visualization claim to show? |
| Evaluation | Can another reviewer test, understand, or challenge the process? |
| Preservation | Are data, model, documentation, and dependencies sustainable? |
| Communication | Is public presentation separated from evidential certainty? |
