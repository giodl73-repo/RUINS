# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS audit the transparency of virtual reconstruction claims?

## Scope

- Use the Seville Principles and London Charter as method anchors.
- Separate source documentation, metadata, paradata, uncertainty, visualization,
  public communication, reuse, and preservation.
- Activate paradata transparency custody.

## Out Of Scope

- No implementation of metadata standards.
- No claim that guideline compliance proves historical correctness.
- No scoring of a specific 3D reconstruction project yet.
