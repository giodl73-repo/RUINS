# Source Lock

Locked claims:

- The Seville Principles identify metadata and paradata as crucial to scientific
  transparency in virtual archaeology.
- London Charter materials frame computer-based visualization processes and
  outcomes as needing to be understood and evaluated.
- Virtual archaeology transparency includes sources, documentation, methods,
  preservation, communication, and reviewability.

Future-locked:

- Exact compliance status of any specific reconstruction project.
- Technical metadata schema implementation.
- Historical correctness of any visualization.
