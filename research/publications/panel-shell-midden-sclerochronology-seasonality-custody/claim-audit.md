# Claim Audit

| Claim | Grade | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Shell midden sclerochronology is a RUINS seasonality custody case. | Strong | It reconstructs seasonality from accretionary shell evidence. |
| Shell isotopes can support bounded shell death or harvest-season claims. | Strong | This is the method claim under habitat, baseline, and edge controls. |
| Shell edge isotope gives exact harvest day. | Refused | Seasonality models carry calibration and growth uncertainty. |
| A few shells are a complete occupation calendar. | Refused | Assemblage sampling and deposition scale are separate. |
| Seasonal harvest proves permanent settlement. | Refused | Settlement permanence requires independent occupation evidence. |
| Shell seasonality is whole diet. | Refused | Diet requires broader faunal, botanical, and residue evidence. |
| Shell temperatures are complete climate reconstruction. | Refused | Baselines, habitat, seasonality, and climate synthesis are separate. |
