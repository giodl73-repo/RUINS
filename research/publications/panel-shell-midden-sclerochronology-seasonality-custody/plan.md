# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use shell isotope seasonality without turning a few shells into a
complete calendar, diet, climate record, or coastal lifeway?

## Scope

- Use shell midden sclerochronology reviews as method trigger.
- Use oxygen-isotope seasonality case studies as harvest-season pressure.
- Use cooking/clumped-isotope studies as alteration pressure.
- Separate specimen, growth, isotope system, habitat, baseline, alteration,
  death season, assemblage, synthesis, and memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact harvest day.
- No complete annual occupation calendar.
- No permanent settlement, whole diet, mobility route, or social schedule.
- No complete climate reconstruction or resource-depletion proof.
