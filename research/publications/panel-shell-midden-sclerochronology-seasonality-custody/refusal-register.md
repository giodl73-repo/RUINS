# Refusal Register

- Do not treat shell edge isotope as exact harvest day.
- Do not treat a small shell sample as a complete annual occupation calendar.
- Do not infer permanent settlement from seasonal harvest alone.
- Do not turn shell midden seasonality into whole diet.
- Do not use shell isotope temperatures as complete climate reconstruction.
- Do not treat shell counts as resource depletion proof.
- Do not turn seasonality patterns into mobility routes or social schedules.
