**Owner:** PALIMPSEST / ERRATA

complete digital edition, language inventory, manuscript catalogue, imaging
method paper, or history of Sinai.

## Future Exact-Claim Minimum

Before RUINS makes stronger claims, it must record manuscript identifiers,
undertext/overtext identity, language and script custody, processing status,
catalogue metadata, edition/transcription source, conservation handling limits,
and sibling handoffs for textual, manuscript, material, imaging, historical,
and geographic claims.
