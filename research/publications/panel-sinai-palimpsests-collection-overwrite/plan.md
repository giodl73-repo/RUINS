# Plan: Sinai Palimpsests Collection Overwrite

**Owner:** ASHLAR
**Status:** publication-ready
**Date:** 2026-07-06

## Problem

The Sinai Palimpsests Project is not a single recovered text. It is a
collection-scale recovery effort across fragile manuscripts, multiple erased
languages, spectral capture, image processing, codicological description,
scholarly identification, and digital catalog publication.

## RUINS Claim

Sinai tests palimpsest overwrite custody beyond Archimedes. A public digital
library, spectral image set, processed image, catalogue record, or discovery
count does not equal a complete edition, translation, textual history, or
author/date identification.

## Work Surface

1. Lock project-scale claims: St. Catherine's, more than 160 palimpsests, erased
   texts from the 4th to 12th century.
2. Lock EMEL claims: 74 palimpsests, 6,800 pages, 305 erased texts, 10
   languages, online digital library.
3. Lock technology claims: reflectance, fluorescence, transmission, raking
   illumination, raw images, automated/manual processing, fragile handling.
4. Build a collection-scale palimpsest overwrite gate.
5. Promote `ruins-capability:palimpsest-overwrite-custody` to Core with a
   checklist and rubric cap.

## Future P3

- exact manuscript-level cataloguing;
- language-specific text identification;
- edition and translation status;
- imaging algorithm and processing audit;
- conservation and handling protocols.
