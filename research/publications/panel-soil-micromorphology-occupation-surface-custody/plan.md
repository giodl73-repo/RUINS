# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use soil micromorphology without turning thin sections into complete
room functions, domestic scenes, or site-use maps?

## Scope

- Use occupation-surface and floor-formation papers as activity-area pressure.
- Use experimental hut floors as analogue and equifinality pressure.
- Use hearth, dung, ash, plant-remain, and geoarchaeology sources as residue pressure.
- Separate block sample, context, microfacies, sediment source, deposition,
  alteration, residue, cleaning, sampling, synthesis, and memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact activity area, complete room function, household biography,
  abandonment episode, cleaning routine, craft workshop, storage area, penning
  system, ritual deposit, or complete site-use map.
