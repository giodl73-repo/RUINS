# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use use-wear and residue evidence without turning surface traces into
complete function, task, diet, hunting, craft, or behavior histories?

## Scope

- Use "Making Sense of Residues on Flaked Stone Artefacts" as the primary
  residue-caution trigger.
- Use ambiguity and quantitative use-wear papers as method pressure.
- Separate artefact, edge, raw material, taphonomy, cleaning, trace formation,
  residue assignment, experiment, action, scale, and memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact function from polish alone.
- No single worked species from residue morphology alone.
- No hunting event or full diet from blood, collagen, starch, or phytoliths.
- No site activity map or skill biography without sibling-owned synthesis.
