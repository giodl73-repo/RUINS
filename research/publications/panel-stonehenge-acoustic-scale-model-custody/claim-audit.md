# Claim Audit

| Claim | Grade | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Stonehenge is an acoustic-space reconstruction custody case. | Strong | Sources anchor reconstructed geometry, scale model, measurement, and inference. |
| The model recovers exact ancient sound. | Refused | Voices, instruments, weather, crowds, and ritual performance are absent. |
| The model supports speech/music acoustic inference. | Strong | Journal abstract anchors measured reverberation and amplification. |
| Stonehenge was designed primarily for acoustics. | Refused | Measurement does not prove builder intention. |
| The model solves ritual function. | Refused | Acoustic effects inform interpretation but do not settle use. |
| Public audio demos are direct ancient hearing. | Refused | They are access layers and simulations. |
