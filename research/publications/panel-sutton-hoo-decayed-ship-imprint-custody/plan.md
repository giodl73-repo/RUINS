# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS score an organic structure when the original fabric is gone and
public knowledge depends on soil imprint, fasteners, associated objects, and
replica/display surfaces?

## Scope

- Sutton Hoo King's Mound / Great Ship Burial.
- Ship imprint and decayed oak problem.
- Missing body and phosphate inference.
- Chamber goods and British Museum display.
- National Trust site, mounds, sculpture, and public-memory framing.

## Out Of Scope

- Complete shipbuilding reconstruction.
- Exact object catalogue.
- Named identity proof.
- Full Anglo-Saxon or East Anglian history.
- Beowulf or literary comparison as evidence.

## Deliverables

- Publication draft.
- Decayed-organic imprint gate.
- Source lock and page-anchor audit.
- Claim audit and refusal register.
- Role review and scorecard.
- Candidate-to-active capability promotion.
