# Refusal Register

RUINS refuses these current-scope claims:

1. The soil imprint is the complete original ship.
2. Rivet pattern alone gives a complete shipbuilding plan.
3. The missing body can be named with certainty.
4. Phosphate evidence supplies biography, cause of death, or identity.
5. The grave goods supply a full ritual script.
6. Replica, sculpture, or display equals the original vessel.
7. Royal or Beowulf-style memory proves named actors or events.
8. The Sutton Hoo treasure gives a total account of Anglo-Saxon society.
