# Claim Audit

| Claim | Grade | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Takarkori ceramic lipid residues are a RUINS ceramic-lipid custody case. | Strong | The case reconstructs food processing from absorbed pottery residues. |
| Ceramic lipids can support bounded dairy or commodity-class claims. | Strong | Biomarker and isotope evidence can identify classes under controls. |
| A pottery residue is an exact meal. | Refused | Absorbed residues are accumulated and transformed chemical traces. |
| Dairy fat proves direct individual diet. | Refused | Vessel contents and bodily consumption are different inference scales. |
| Vessel residues reconstruct a complete recipe. | Refused | Ingredients, proportions, sequence, and preparation remain unproven. |
| Milk processing proves a whole herd economy. | Refused | Herd structure and economy require animal, settlement, and synthesis evidence. |
| First-dairying memory is complete foodway history. | Refused | Public priority framing is not a complete subsistence model. |
