# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use ceramic lipid residues without turning absorbed fats into exact
meals, recipes, herd economies, or complete foodway histories?

## Scope

- Use the Takarkori/Libyan Sahara dairy-fat paper as primary trigger.
- Use archaeological lipid-residue method papers as control pressure.
- Separate vessel, sherd, residue location, preservation, extraction,
  biomarker assignment, isotope reference range, commodity class, vessel use,
  subsistence scale, sibling synthesis, and memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact meal or complete recipe from absorbed residue.
- No direct individual diet from a vessel signal.
- No whole herd economy or domestication proof without FAUNA/MAXIM synthesis.
- No trade route, ethnicity, or climate proxy without sibling-owned controls.
