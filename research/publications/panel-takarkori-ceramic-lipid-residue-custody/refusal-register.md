# Refusal Register

- Do not treat absorbed pottery lipid as an exact meal.
- Do not treat dairy-fat assignment as direct individual diet.
- Do not infer complete recipes from fatty-acid or isotope profiles.
- Do not make whole herd economy or domestication claims from pottery alone.
- Do not turn isotope ranges into ethnic identity, migration, or trade routes.
- Do not use ceramic residues as climate proxies without separate controls.
- Do not let first-dairying headlines become complete foodway history.
