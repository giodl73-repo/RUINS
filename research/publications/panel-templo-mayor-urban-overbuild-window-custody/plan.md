# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS score a buried urban site when the public evidence is mediated
by excavation windows, museum routes, under-building fragments, and continuing
urban archaeology?

## Scope

- Templo Mayor Museum and site custody.
- UNESCO overbuilt-city frame for Mexico City / Tenochtitlan.
- Huei Tzompantli as an under-building excavation-window example.
- Urban Archaeology Program as the custody mechanism for ongoing discoveries.

## Out Of Scope

- Full Mexica ritual interpretation.
- Complete Sacred Precinct reconstruction.
- Complete Tenochtitlan city plan.
- Exact object cataloguing beyond source-locked examples.
- Conservation engineering instructions.

## Deliverables

- Publication draft.
- Urban-overbuild window gate.
- Source lock and page-anchor audit.
- Claim audit and refusal register.
- Role review and scorecard.
- Candidate-to-active capability promotion.
