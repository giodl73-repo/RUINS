# Refusal Register

RUINS refuses these current-scope claims:

1. The excavation window is the whole buried city.
2. The visible Huei Tzompantli fragment is the complete structure.
3. The museum route gives direct access to unmediated original urban fabric.
4. PAU discoveries solve the full Sacred Precinct inventory.
5. Removed objects preserve their complete original contexts without separate
   excavation custody.
6. Digital scans or replicas are object-equivalent.
7. Public rediscovery memory proves original ritual meaning.
8. Conservation monitoring means conservation is finished.
