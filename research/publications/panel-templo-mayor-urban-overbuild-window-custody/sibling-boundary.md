# Sibling Boundary

| Surface | Owner | RUINS boundary |
|---|---|---|
| Mexico City / Tenochtitlan geography | PORTO | RUINS only grades overbuild and exposure custody. |
| Named structures, deities, monoliths, and objects | CANON | RUINS does not become the identity registry. |
| Stone, stucco, bone, soil, foundation, and conservation science | CERES | RUINS records conservation as custody, not material science. |
| Ritual, sacrifice, deity meaning, and calendrical interpretation | RITE | RUINS refuses final ritual interpretation. |
| Mexica, colonial, and national history | MAXIM / LUCIA | RUINS keeps history as context and memory, not total synthesis. |
| Property, construction, public access, and governance | Policy / urban systems | RUINS does not issue management or legal guidance. |
| Digital documentation and replicas | Digital heritage systems | RUINS treats scans as mediated access layers. |
