## Current Refusals

1. Do not infer complete identity or biography from the preserved face.
2. Do not collapse death cause into motive.
3. Do not treat last meal as ritual proof.
4. Do not treat display state as original body state.
5. Do not generalize one bog body to all Iron Age bodies.
