**Owner:** PALIMPSEST / ERRATA

## Result

The current paper claims have public anchors. Remaining exact-claim work is
future-locked rather than a publication blocker for this scope:

1. no complete ancient or modern recipe,
2. no universal production process across sites, species, or periods,
3. no exact chemical concentration, sample-value, or species-chemistry claim
   without CERES custody,
4. no Pliny, Pollux, or other ancient passage quotation without FONTES/MUNDUS
   custody,
5. no production scale from shell heaps alone,
6. no social-status inference from color alone.
