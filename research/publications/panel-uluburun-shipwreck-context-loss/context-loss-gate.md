# Context-Loss Gate: Uluburun Shipwreck

**Owner:** ASHLAR / PALIMPSEST / ERRATA
**Status:** activation-ready capability gate
**Date:** 2026-07-05

This gate tests `ruins-capability:shipwreck-context-loss`. It separates what a
shipwreck cargo can establish from what it cannot establish without route,
destination, ownership, cargo-function, and operating-system custody.

## Future Exact-Claim Minimum

Before RUINS adds stronger shipwreck-context claims, it must record:

1. Excavation publication or catalogue source.
2. Object, cargo class, or assemblage unit.
3. Whether the claim concerns site position, stowage, cargo, hull, route,
   destination, owner, crew, or later display.
4. Whether the source is observation, analysis, reconstruction, analogy, or
   public summary.
5. Sibling handoff if the claim belongs to PORTO, CANON, FONTES, MAXIM, LUCIA,
   or CERES rather than RUINS.

## Decision

This gate has enough source custody for the current draft and is ready for a
role-panel activation decision. Exact campaign, object, route, destination,
owner, crew, hull, and stowage claims remain locked behind future direct
publication or catalogue custody.
