**Owner:** PALIMPSEST / ERRATA
**Status:** current-draft custody boundary closed
**Date:** 2026-07-05

This audit closes the current-draft blocker around Pulak 1998 and the campaign
reports. It does so by separating what the present paper uses from what it still
refuses.

route, destination, owner, crew, or stowage claims. Therefore direct Pulak 1998
overview custody is sufficient for the current context-loss argument, while
campaign reports remain future exact-claim sources.
