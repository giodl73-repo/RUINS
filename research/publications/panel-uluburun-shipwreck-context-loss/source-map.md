## Evidence Surfaces

| Surface | Evidence condition | RUINS use |
|---|---|---|
| Wreck site | excavated underwater context | Establish shipwreck setting and recovery conditions. |
| Cargo assemblage | recovered material / conservation-mediated | Track object survival and cargo-pattern claims. |
| Hull and anchors | partial ship-operation traces | Bound vessel and construction claims. |
| Excavation publications | archaeological custody | Support excavation, cargo, and interpretation claims. |
| Scientific analyses | sample/method custody | Support metal, glass, resin, wood, and dating claims only where source-locked. |
| Museum display | modern custody / public memory | Track access and afterlife, not original context. |
| Route and trade models | reconstruction / synthesis | Treat as hypotheses unless source-specific. |
