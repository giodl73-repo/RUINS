# Chronology: Vinland Saga Site Crosscheck

**Owner:** CALENDAR
**Status:** working chronology

| Layer | Event/process | RUINS significance |
|---|---|---|
| ca. AD 1000-1021 | Norse activity at L'Anse aux Meadows. | Archaeological and scientific dating layer. |
| AD 1021 | Nature dating anchors Norse activity at the site. | Site date, not all saga events. |
| 13th century and later textual transmission | Vinland sagas are written down after oral/literary transmission. | Textual memory layer. |
| 1960s | L'Anse aux Meadows is identified and excavated as a Norse site. | Modern archaeological custody. |
| 1978 | UNESCO World Heritage inscription. | Public/institutional custody. |
| Present | Parks Canada interpretation and living-history framing. | Cultural-memory afterlife. |

## Chronology Rule

Do not collapse Norse activity, saga writing, excavation, scientific dating,
World Heritage inscription, and public interpretation into one event.
