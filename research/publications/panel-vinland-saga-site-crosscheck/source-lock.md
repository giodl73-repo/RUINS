## Paper Use Rules

1. Keep site, saga, route, name, date, actor, and public-memory claims separate.
2. Do not quote saga passages without edition/translation custody.
3. Do not identify L'Anse aux Meadows as Vinland without qualification.
4. Do not use AD 1021 to date all saga events.
5. Do not use Norse evidence to erase Indigenous presence or histories.
