# Light-Mediated Gate

| Layer | VR custody question |
|---|---|
| Source object | Which cave wall, art surface, or plaquette is modelled? |
| Capture | What scan or photogrammetry method produced the model? |
| Software | What engine, lighting model, and assumptions mediate the scene? |
| Viewer | Which height, distance, movement, and angle are simulated? |
| Light | What torch/fire behavior is modelled and what is omitted? |
| Visibility | What visual effect is observed under the simulation? |
| Inference | Does the claim stop before intention, ritual, and exact perception? |
| Access | Is VR treated as presentation and analysis, not original experience? |
