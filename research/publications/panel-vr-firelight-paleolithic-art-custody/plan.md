# Plan

## Question

Can light-mediated reconstruction custody survive a shift from live combustion
experiments to VR/digital firelight?

## Scope

- Use Wisher 2023 and repository/abstract metadata as source anchors.
- Separate scans, models, software, viewing positions, dynamic light, visibility,
  sensory hypothesis, presentation, and memory.
- Promote the capability to Core with rubric v1.32 and a checklist.

## Out Of Scope

- No Unity implementation.
- No final interpretation of Las Monedas or Montastruc imagery.
- No claim of recovered Paleolithic perception.
