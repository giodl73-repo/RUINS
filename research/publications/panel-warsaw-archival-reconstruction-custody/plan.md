# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS score destroyed urban fabric when it is rebuilt from surviving
fabric, archival documents, iconography, inventories, expert interpretation, and
post-war symbolic memory?

## Scope

- UNESCO Historic Centre of Warsaw World Heritage page.
- 1945-1951 Warsaw Reconstruction Office project.
- Use of reliable archival documents, iconography, inventories, and surviving
  fabric.
- Deliberate adaptations, selective non-rebuilding, and interior changes.
- World Heritage and Memory of the World recognition.

## Out Of Scope

- Complete Warsaw wartime history.
- Exact building-by-building reconstruction audit.
- Legal/political analysis of post-war governance.
- Technical construction methods.
- Total conservation doctrine history.

## Deliverables

- Publication draft.
- Archival-reconstruction gate.
- Source lock and page-anchor audit.
- Claim audit and refusal register.
- Role review and scorecard.
- Candidate-to-active capability promotion.
