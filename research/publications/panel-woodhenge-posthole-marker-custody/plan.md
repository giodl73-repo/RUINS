# Plan

## Question

How should RUINS score a vanished timber monument when public knowledge depends
on postholes, concrete markers, crop marks, deposited finds, and uncertain
ritual/function interpretation?

## Scope

- Woodhenge discovery by aerial photography.
- Postholes, six timber ovals, bank and ditch.
- Concrete visitor markers.
- Offerings/deposits and lost child-burial remains.
- UNESCO landscape statement about disappeared timber structures and postholes.

## Out Of Scope

- Complete timber-building reconstruction.
- Final ritual or ceremonial function.
- Exact post-by-post technical report.
- Full Stonehenge/Avebury landscape synthesis.
- Solstice meaning beyond source-scoped alignment.

## Deliverables

- Publication draft.
- Posthole-marker gate.
- Source lock and page-anchor audit.
- Claim audit and refusal register.
- Role review and scorecard.
- Active-to-Core capability promotion.
