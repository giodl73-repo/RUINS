# Posthole Marker Gate

| Layer | Can support | Cannot support alone |
|---|---|---|
| Crop marks | Discovery and buried-feature signal. | Complete monument plan. |
| Postholes | Timber-post location and pattern. | Original post height, decoration, roof, or full building. |
| Concrete markers | Public orientation and display. | Original timber equivalence. |
| Bank and ditch | Henge setting and earthwork survival. | Complete ritual route or final function. |
| Finds and offerings | Depositional practices. | Complete ceremony or social system. |
| Lost child remains | Excavation and custody loss. | Complete person identity or sacrifice narrative. |
| Solstice alignment | Alignment evidence. | Final ceremonial meaning. |
| World Heritage landscape | Broader protected context. | Whole Stonehenge/Avebury interpretation. |
