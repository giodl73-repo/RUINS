# Plan

## Question

Can RUINS use bone modifications and mortality profiles without turning traces
on bones into complete subsistence, ritual, or human-evolution stories?

## Scope

- Use cut-mark and tooth-mark papers as behavior-inference pressure.
- Use trampling, knapping, and carnivore-damage studies as equifinality pressure.
- Use mortality-profile studies as kill-off and herd-management pressure.
- Separate specimen, taxon, surface, mark, actor, timing, assemblage, mortality,
  analogue, synthesis, and memory.

## Out Of Scope

- No exact butchery event, certain hunting/scavenging behavior, complete diet,
  ritual feast, species-specific predator, tool type, site function, or complete
  subsistence system.
